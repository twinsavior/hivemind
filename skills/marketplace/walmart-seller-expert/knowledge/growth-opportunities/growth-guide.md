# Walmart Marketplace Growth Opportunities Guide

## Overview

Walmart Marketplace offers multiple programs and tools to help sellers grow their business, increase visibility, and improve conversion rates. This guide covers the Pro Seller badge program, promotional opportunities, review programs, search optimization, and pricing strategies.

---

## Pro Seller Program

### Overview
The Pro Seller program automatically rewards sellers meeting performance metrics with tiered status levels. Pro Sellers achieve 1.7x the conversion rate of the average Marketplace seller. Status is evaluated and refreshed on the 5th and 20th of every month.

### Tier Structure

The program has three tiers: Rising Seller, Advanced Seller, and Pro Seller. Sellers are automatically awarded the highest tier they qualify for.

#### Rising Seller Requirements

| Metric | Threshold |
|--------|-----------|
| On-Time Delivery | Greater than or equal to 90% |
| Cancellation Rate | Less than or equal to 2.5% |
| Seller Response Rate | Greater than or equal to 90% |
| Shipping Speed Score | Greater than or equal to 5% |
| Content Quality Score | Greater than or equal to 65% |
| Price Competitiveness | Greater than or equal to 65% |
| Orders in last 90 days | 100+ |
| Active days | 90+ |
| Policy compliance | Full compliance |

#### Advanced Seller Requirements
All Rising Seller requirements, PLUS stricter thresholds:

| Metric | Threshold |
|--------|-----------|
| On-Time Delivery | Greater than or equal to 92.5% |
| Cancellation Rate | Less than or equal to 2.0% |
| Seller Response Rate | Greater than or equal to 92.5% |
| Shipping Speed Score | Greater than or equal to 25% |
| Content Quality Score | Greater than or equal to 70% |
| Price Competitiveness | Greater than or equal to 70% |

#### Pro Seller Requirements
All Rising Seller requirements, PLUS the highest thresholds:

| Metric | Threshold |
|--------|-----------|
| On-Time Delivery | Greater than or equal to 95% |
| Cancellation Rate | Less than or equal to 1.5% |
| Seller Response Rate | Greater than or equal to 95% |
| Shipping Speed Score | Greater than or equal to 50% |
| Content Quality Score | Greater than or equal to 75% |
| Price Competitiveness | Greater than or equal to 75% |

### Benefits by Tier

#### Rising Seller Benefits
- Up to 25% discount on Ship with Walmart labels
- Up to 10% increase on Faster Payout balance

#### Advanced Seller Benefits
- 5% referral fee discount on Pro Listings
- $50 monthly Post-Purchase Reviews credit
- Ship with Walmart discounts
- Faster Payouts access
- Exclusive Pro Seller Master Series webinars

#### Pro Seller Benefits
- $50 monthly Search Engine Marketing credit
- 10% referral fee discount on Pro Listings
- Special Walmart Marketplace Capital terms
- $50 monthly Post-Purchase Reviews credit
- Ship with Walmart discounts
- Faster Payouts access
- Flash Deals access
- Extra Savings access
- Pro Seller Badge displayed on listings

### Status Maintenance
- Status refreshes on the 5th and 20th of every month
- Sellers lose status after falling below criteria for two consecutive refresh cycles
- Post-Purchase Reviews and SEM credits expire three months after issuance
- Sellers receiving policy warnings may become ineligible until violations are resolved
- Walmart reserves the right to verify eligibility, recalculate, or reassess incentive benefits

## New-Seller Savings Program

### Overview
Walmart offers significant fee discounts for new sellers to reduce the barrier to entry.

### 2026 Benefits
- **25% off fulfillment fees** (for WFS users)
- **50% off storage fees** (for WFS users)
- **Up to $2,000 in total savings**

### Eligibility
- Available to newly onboarded Marketplace sellers
- Applies to WFS fulfillment and storage fees
- Time-limited program (check current terms for expiration)

## Promotional Pricing

### Flash Deals
- Available to Pro Seller tier members
- Time-limited, deeply discounted offers that drive high visibility
- Appear in dedicated promotional placements on Walmart.com

### Extra Savings
- Available to Pro Seller tier members
- Special pricing events that highlight value to customers

### Promotional Pricing Tools
- Set promotional item prices for events such as clearance sales through the Promotions API
- Manage pricing through Seller Center's pricing management tools
- Use the Repricer for automated competitive pricing adjustments

### Sales Events
- Walmart hosts seasonal and promotional sales events throughout the year
- Sellers can participate by offering competitive pricing during events
- Event participation can significantly boost visibility and sales volume

## Marketplace Reviews Program

### Post-Purchase Reviews
- Program that helps sellers gather authentic product reviews
- Advanced and Pro Sellers receive $50 monthly credit toward the program
- Credits expire three months after issuance
- Reviews are collected from verified purchasers and displayed on product listings

### Review Impact
- Reviews significantly influence purchase decisions and conversion rates
- Products with more reviews tend to rank higher in search results
- Authentic reviews improve listing quality scores

### Review Guidelines
- Walmart has review sampling guidelines that sellers must follow
- Inflating or manipulating customer reviews is prohibited under the Code of Conduct
- Only authentic, organic reviews from verified purchasers are acceptable

## Search Insights Tool

### Overview
The Search Insights tool helps sellers understand how customers find products and optimize listings for better visibility.

### Capabilities
- View trending search terms in your product categories
- Identify high-traffic keywords relevant to your products
- Analyze search volume and competition for specific terms
- Understand seasonal search trends

### Using Insights for Optimization
- Incorporate high-performing keywords into product titles and descriptions
- Identify gaps in your catalog based on customer search behavior
- Time promotions around peak search periods
- Adjust pricing based on competitive search landscape

## Listing Quality Optimization

### Content Quality Score
- Impacts Pro Seller eligibility (65% minimum for Rising, 70% for Advanced, 75% for Pro)
- Measures the completeness and quality of product listings
- Higher scores correlate with better search visibility and conversion

### Optimization Best Practices
- Use clear, descriptive product titles with relevant keywords
- Provide complete and accurate product attributes
- Include high-quality images from multiple angles
- Write detailed product descriptions addressing customer questions
- Specify all relevant product specifications
- Include variant information (size, color, etc.) accurately

### Image Guidelines
- Primary image is required for publishing
- Missing primary images will cause items to be unpublished
- Follow Walmart's specific image requirements for dimensions and quality
- Multiple images improve conversion rates

### Price Competitiveness Score
- Impacts Pro Seller eligibility (65% minimum for Rising, 70% for Advanced, 75% for Pro)
- Measures how competitive your pricing is relative to the market
- Includes both item price and shipping costs

## Pricing Strategies

### Repricer Tool
- Automated pricing tool that adjusts prices based on competitive conditions
- Multiple strategy options available for different competitive scenarios
- Helps maintain Buy Box eligibility through competitive pricing

### Pricing Insights Dashboard
- View competitor pricing for similar products
- Identify pricing opportunities in your categories
- Track pricing trends over time

### Buy Box Optimization
- Price competitiveness is a key factor in winning the Buy Box
- Total price (item + shipping) is evaluated
- Free shipping or competitive shipping rates improve Buy Box chances
- WFS fulfillment can provide an advantage

### Shipping Speed Score
- Impacts Pro Seller eligibility (5% minimum for Rising, 25% for Advanced, 50% for Pro)
- Faster shipping speeds improve customer satisfaction and conversion
- Consider using Ship with Walmart for discounted shipping labels
- WFS provides automatic fast shipping through Walmart's network

## Key Growth Tips

1. **Achieve Pro Seller status** for maximum benefits including the 10% referral fee discount and Pro Seller badge
2. **Optimize listings** with complete attributes, quality images, and competitive pricing
3. **Use WFS** to improve shipping speed and let Walmart handle most performance metrics
4. **Leverage the Repricer** to maintain competitive pricing automatically
5. **Participate in promotional events** like Flash Deals and Extra Savings
6. **Collect reviews** through the Post-Purchase Reviews program
7. **Use Search Insights** to optimize listings for high-traffic keywords
8. **Take advantage of New-Seller Savings** if you are a new seller
9. **Monitor Content Quality and Price Competitiveness scores** to maintain Pro Seller eligibility
10. **Invest in advertising** through Walmart Connect to boost visibility
