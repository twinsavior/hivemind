# eBay Buyer Communication and Messaging Guide

> Complete reference for the eBay messaging system, response time expectations, allowed and prohibited communication, feedback management, Best Offer negotiations, and handling buyer requests.

---

## eBay Messaging System Overview

All buyer-seller communication on eBay happens through eBay's internal messaging system. This protects both parties and creates a record eBay can reference in dispute resolution.

### Accessing Messages

```
Seller Hub > Messages
```
Or:
```
My eBay > Messages
```
Or:
```
eBay Seller App > Messages tab
```

### Message Types

| Type | Description | Where It Appears |
|---|---|---|
| **Buyer inquiry** | Pre-sale question about a listing | Messages inbox, linked to listing |
| **Order message** | Post-sale communication about a specific order | Messages inbox, linked to order |
| **Best Offer notification** | Buyer made an offer on your listing | Messages + Seller Hub > Offers |
| **Return/case message** | Communication within a return or case | Messages + Resolution Center |
| **Combined shipping request** | Buyer asks to combine orders | Messages inbox |
| **eBay system message** | Automated notifications (sale, payment, policy) | Messages inbox (from "eBay") |

## Response Time Expectations

### How Response Time Is Measured

eBay tracks how quickly you respond to buyer messages. While response time is not currently a direct seller level metric, it affects:
- Buyer satisfaction and likelihood of purchase
- Likelihood of cases being opened (frustrated buyers escalate faster)
- Potential future metric (eBay has tested response time requirements)

### Recommended Response Windows

| Message Type | Target Response Time | Maximum Before Risk |
|---|---|---|
| Pre-sale question | Within 4 hours | 24 hours |
| Post-sale inquiry | Within 4 hours | 24 hours |
| Return request | Within 24 hours | 3 business days (eBay deadline) |
| Case/dispute message | Within 12 hours | 3 business days (eBay deadline) |
| Best Offer | Within 24 hours | 48 hours (offer may expire) |
| Combined shipping request | Within 12 hours | 24 hours (before buyer pays separately) |

> **Best practice**: Aim to respond to all messages within 12 hours during business days. Use the eBay Seller app to get push notifications and respond from your phone.

### Setting Up Notifications

```
My eBay > Account > Communication Preferences > Notification Preferences
```

Enable notifications for:
- New messages from buyers
- New orders
- Return requests opened
- Cases opened
- Best Offer received
- Items sold

## What You CAN and CANNOT Say in Messages

eBay has strict rules about what sellers can communicate to buyers through the messaging system.

### Prohibited Communication

| Prohibited Action | Details | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| **Sharing email address** | Cannot provide your email or ask for buyer's email | Account restriction or suspension |
| **Sharing phone number** | Cannot provide your phone number (unless for pickup arrangements) | Account restriction |
| **Linking to external websites** | Cannot include URLs to your website, Amazon, social media | Message blocked or account warning |
| **Offering off-eBay transactions** | Cannot suggest completing the sale outside eBay | Account suspension |
| **Sending marketing emails** | Cannot use eBay messages for promotional content | Spam violation |
| **Threatening buyers** | No threats, harassment, or abusive language | Immediate account restriction |
| **Sharing other buyer's information** | Cannot reveal other buyers' names, addresses, or order details | Privacy violation |
| **Including links to non-eBay payment methods** | No Venmo, Zelle, CashApp, direct PayPal links | Account suspension |

### Allowed Communication

| Allowed Action | Best Practices |
|---|---|
| Answering product questions | Be thorough, honest, and timely |
| Providing shipping updates | Share tracking info, expected delivery dates |
| Discussing return details | Keep it professional, reference eBay policies |
| Negotiating offers | Use the Best Offer system, be respectful |
| Requesting buyer feedback | Polite, non-pressuring, follow eBay guidelines |
| Sending combined shipping invoices | Use the "Send Invoice" feature |
| Sharing photos of item | Attach photos to messages for additional angles/details |
| Providing care/usage instructions | Helpful for buyer experience |

### Message Auto-Filtering

eBay automatically scans and may block messages containing:
- Email addresses (name@domain.com patterns)
- Phone numbers
- External URLs (non-ebay.com links)
- Certain keywords associated with off-platform transactions

> **Tip**: If eBay blocks your message, rephrase without the flagged content. If you need to communicate about a legitimate eBay-related matter that keeps getting blocked, contact eBay support.

## Feedback System

### How eBay Feedback Works

| Aspect | Details |
|---|---|
| **Who can leave feedback** | Buyers can leave positive, neutral, or negative. Sellers can only leave positive for buyers. |
| **Timing** | Buyer can leave feedback within 60 days of transaction. Seller can leave feedback within 60 days. |
| **What's shown** | Star rating (1-5) for: item description accuracy, communication, shipping time, shipping cost. Plus a text comment. |
| **Impact** | Negative/neutral feedback lowers your feedback score and percentage |

### Feedback Score Calculation

| Feedback Type | Score Impact |
|---|---|
| Positive | +1 |
| Neutral | 0 |
| Negative | -1 |

Your **feedback percentage** = (Positives / Total Feedback) x 100

| Feedback Percentage | Buyer Perception |
|---|---|
| 99.5% - 100% | Excellent — buyers trust fully |
| 98% - 99.4% | Good — most buyers comfortable |
| 95% - 97.9% | Acceptable — some buyers may hesitate |
| Below 95% | Concerning — significant buyer hesitation, search impact |

## Handling Negative Feedback

### Step 1: Evaluate the Feedback

| Question | If Yes | If No |
|---|---|---|
| Is the feedback factually accurate? | Accept it, improve your processes | Proceed to request revision or removal |
| Did you make a mistake? | Own it, respond professionally | Document why it's inaccurate |
| Does it violate eBay's feedback policy? | Request automatic removal | Attempt revision request first |

### Step 2: Respond to the Feedback

You can leave a **public reply** to any feedback. This response is visible to all buyers viewing your profile.

```
My eBay > Account > Feedback > Feedback received > Reply
```

**Good response examples:**
- "We apologize for the shipping delay. A full refund was issued and we have improved our handling process."
- "This buyer received a full refund on [date]. We stand behind our 30-day return policy."
- "We shipped within our stated handling time. Tracking number [XXX] confirms timely delivery."

**Bad response examples:**
- "This buyer is a liar and a scammer!" (never attack the buyer)
- "Not our fault the postal service is slow" (deflects responsibility)
- "Whatever" (unprofessional)

### Step 3: Request Feedback Revision

You can send a buyer a **feedback revision request** up to 5 times per 1,000 feedback received (rolling 12 months).

```
Seller Hub > Performance > Feedback > Request Feedback Revision
```

| Guideline | Details |
|---|---|
| Maximum revision requests | 5 per 1,000 feedback (12-month rolling) |
| Can you contact buyer first? | Yes — resolve the issue, then request revision |
| Buyer obligation to revise? | None — it's entirely voluntary |
| Time limit for buyer to respond | 10 days after request is sent |
| Can buyer decline? | Yes — and it counts against your revision request quota |

**Best approach:**
1. Message the buyer first to resolve the underlying issue
2. Offer a solution (partial refund, replacement, full refund)
3. Once resolved, politely ask if they would consider revising their feedback
4. If they agree verbally, send the formal revision request through eBay
5. Never pressure, bribe, or threaten

### Step 4: Request Automatic Removal

eBay automatically removes feedback that violates their feedback policy.

### Criteria for Automatic Feedback Removal

| Violation | Example | Removal Likely? |
|---|---|---|
| **Contains profanity or hate speech** | Obscene language in the comment | Yes |
| **Contains personal information** | Includes buyer or seller name, address, phone | Yes |
| **Feedback extortion** | "I'll leave negative unless you give me a discount" | Yes (if reported) |
| **Left by a suspended buyer** | Buyer's account was suspended within the feedback period | Automatic |
| **Wrong transaction** | Feedback clearly refers to a different seller or transaction | Yes, with evidence |
| **Threats** | "I'll report you to the FBI" or similar threats | Yes |
| **eBay closed case in seller's favor** | INAD case resolved in your favor but buyer left related negative | Yes (if linked to specific case) |

### How to Request Removal

```
My eBay > Account > Feedback > Feedback received > Report
```

Or:
```
Contact eBay Customer Service > Feedback Dispute
```

Select the reason for removal from the dropdown:
- Contains profanity
- Contains personal information
- Is feedback extortion
- Was left by a suspended user
- Case was resolved in my favor

## Feedback Extortion Policy

eBay has a specific policy against feedback extortion — when a buyer threatens negative feedback to extract concessions.

### What Constitutes Feedback Extortion

| Extortion | Not Extortion |
|---|---|
| "Give me a partial refund or I'll leave negative feedback" | "I'm disappointed with this item and considering leaving feedback about my experience" |
| "Send me a free accessory or I'll rate you 1 star" | "Can I get a small discount since the item has a minor flaw?" |
| "Refund me $50 and I won't report you" | "I'd like to return this item for a refund" |
| "Change my shipping cost to free or I'll leave negative" | "I think the shipping charge was too high compared to actual cost" |

### How to Report Feedback Extortion

1. **Do NOT comply** with the extortion demand
2. **Save the message** — do not delete any communication
3. **Report to eBay**:
```
Seller Hub > Messages > [Open the extortion message] > Report
```
Or:
```
Contact eBay Customer Service > Report a Buyer > Feedback Extortion
```

4. **If negative feedback was already left**: Request removal citing feedback extortion, include the message thread as evidence
5. **eBay's response**: If confirmed as extortion, eBay removes the feedback and may warn or suspend the buyer

> **Important**: The extortion must be explicit in eBay messages. Vague buyer unhappiness is not extortion. eBay looks for a direct conditional threat: "do X or I'll leave negative."

## Best Offer Negotiations

### How Best Offer Works

When enabled on a fixed-price listing, buyers can submit offers below your listing price.

### Best Offer Settings

```
Listing Form > Pricing > Best Offer > Enable
```

| Setting | Description |
|---|---|
| **Auto-accept threshold** | Offers at or above this price are automatically accepted |
| **Auto-decline threshold** | Offers at or below this price are automatically declined |
| **Manual review range** | Offers between auto-decline and auto-accept go to your inbox for manual decision |

### Best Offer Response Options

| Option | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Accept** | Sale completes at the offered price |
| **Decline** | Offer rejected. Buyer can submit another offer. |
| **Counter** | You propose a different price. Buyer can accept, decline, or counter back. |
| **Let it expire** | Offers expire after 48 hours if not responded to |

### Best Offer Negotiation Strategies

| Strategy | Details |
|---|---|
| **Set auto-accept at your minimum** | Saves time on acceptable offers |
| **Set auto-decline at lowball threshold** | Don't waste time on unreasonable offers (e.g., below 60% of asking) |
| **Counter at 90-95% of asking** | Shows willingness to negotiate without giving too much |
| **Track accepted offer prices** | If most offers are at a similar price, consider lowering your listing price |
| **Counter quickly** | Fast responses increase likelihood of completing the sale |
| **Maximum counter-offers** | Each party can make up to 5 counter-offers per negotiation |

### Best Offer Limits

- A buyer can make up to 3 offers on the same listing if declined
- After 3 declined offers, the buyer is blocked from that listing for 3 days
- You can block specific buyers from making offers
- Offers are binding — if you accept, the buyer must pay

## Combined Shipping Requests

### When Buyers Request Combined Shipping

A buyer who purchases (or wants to purchase) multiple items from you may request combined shipping to save on costs.

### How to Handle Combined Shipping Requests

**Before payment:**
1. Buyer messages you asking to combine shipping
2. You agree and tell the buyer to purchase all items
3. After all items are purchased, you **send a combined invoice**:
```
Seller Hub > Orders > [Select orders from same buyer] > Send Invoice
```
4. Adjust the shipping total on the invoice
5. Buyer pays the adjusted invoice amount

**After payment (separate orders already paid):**
1. Ship all items in one package
2. Upload tracking to each order (same tracking number on all)
3. Issue a partial refund for the difference in shipping cost

### Combined Shipping Best Practices

- Mention combined shipping availability in your listings
- Set up combined shipping rules in your shipping profile (automatic discounts)
- Respond to combination requests quickly — before the buyer pays separate shipping on each item
- Always send a combined invoice rather than asking the buyer to overpay and promising a refund

## Cancellation Requests from Buyers

### When a Buyer Asks to Cancel

| Timing | Your Options |
|---|---|
| **Before you ship** | You can cancel without defect if you select "Buyer asked to cancel" as the reason |
| **After you ship** | Cannot cancel — buyer must receive item and then return it |
| **After delivery** | Not a cancellation — buyer should open a return |

### How to Cancel an Order

```
Seller Hub > Orders > [Select order] > Cancel Order
```

**Cancellation reasons and their impact:**

| Reason | Defect? | When to Use |
|---|---|---|
| Buyer asked to cancel | No | Buyer contacted you and requested cancellation |
| Out of stock or damaged | Yes (defect) | You cannot fulfill the order |
| Issue with buyer's address | No | Address is incomplete, PO Box when not supported, etc. |
| Buyer didn't pay | No | Buyer failed to complete payment (rare with immediate payment) |

> **Critical**: Always select the correct reason. Choosing "buyer asked to cancel" when it was actually your decision (out of stock) is a policy violation if eBay investigates.

### Handling Buyer "Remorse" Cancellation Requests

If a buyer asks to cancel simply because they changed their mind:
- You are NOT obligated to cancel
- If you have already shipped, politely inform the buyer that the item is in transit and they can return it after delivery per your return policy
- If you have NOT shipped, it's generally better to cancel (preserves the relationship, avoids negative feedback risk)

## Asking Buyers to Update Feedback

### eBay's Rules on Requesting Feedback

| Allowed | Not Allowed |
|---|---|
| Sending ONE polite follow-up message after delivery | Sending multiple messages requesting feedback |
| Including a thank-you note in the package with a general note about feedback | Including instructions to leave 5-star feedback |
| Sending a feedback revision request through eBay's system | Offering incentives (discounts, freebies) for positive feedback |
| Resolving issues and then asking if buyer would consider updating | Pressuring buyers who left negative/neutral to change it |
| Using eBay's automated feedback reminder (built into Seller Hub) | Using external tools to mass-email buyers requesting feedback |

### Professional Feedback Request Template

After delivery, you may send a message like:

> "Thank you for your purchase! I hope you're enjoying your [item]. If you have a moment, I'd appreciate it if you could leave feedback about your experience. If there's anything about your order that wasn't perfect, please message me first so I can make it right. Thank you for your business!"

### What NOT to Say

- "Please leave 5-star feedback" (specifying the rating is manipulative)
- "I'll leave you positive feedback if you leave me positive" (feedback manipulation)
- "Please don't leave negative feedback — message me first" (implying they shouldn't leave honest feedback)
- "I'll give you a discount on your next purchase if you leave positive" (incentivized feedback)

## Message Templates for Common Scenarios

### Pre-Sale Question Response Structure

1. **Acknowledge the question** — thank them for reaching out
2. **Answer directly** — provide the specific information requested
3. **Add value** — mention relevant details they didn't ask about
4. **Close with availability** — "Feel free to ask if you have any other questions"

### Post-Sale: Shipping Notification

1. **Confirm shipment** — "Your item has been shipped!"
2. **Provide tracking context** — "Tracking may take 24 hours to update"
3. **Set delivery expectation** — "Expected delivery is [date]"
4. **Offer support** — "Please reach out if you have any questions"

### Return Request Response

1. **Acknowledge promptly** — "I received your return request"
2. **Express understanding** — "I understand that [their reason]"
3. **State your action** — "I've approved the return / I'd like to offer..."
4. **Provide next steps** — "You'll receive a return label shortly"

### Negative Feedback Follow-Up

1. **Apologize for their experience** — regardless of fault
2. **Offer a solution** — refund, replacement, partial refund
3. **Do not mention feedback revision** — resolve first, ask later
4. **After resolution** — "I'm glad we could resolve this. If you feel your experience has been addressed, you're welcome to update your feedback, but there's absolutely no obligation."

## Communication Red Flags to Watch For

| Buyer Behavior | What It May Indicate | Recommended Action |
|---|---|---|
| Asks you to ship to a different address | Potential fraud | Only ship to the address on the order |
| Asks to complete transaction outside eBay | Scam attempt | Decline, report to eBay |
| Threatens negative feedback before receiving item | Potential extortion | Document, report if it escalates |
| Asks for your personal email or phone | Against eBay policy | Politely decline, keep communication on eBay |
| Claims item is counterfeit before receiving it | Possible return setup | Provide authenticity documentation proactively |
| Makes multiple low offers then sends aggressive messages | Problematic buyer | Block buyer from your listings |
| Asks you to declare lower customs value | Illegal (customs fraud) | Decline — this is against the law |

### How to Block a Buyer

```
My eBay > Account > Site Preferences > Buyer Requirements > Blocked Buyer List
```

Add the buyer's username to your blocked buyer list. They will not be able to:
- Purchase your items
- Make Best Offers on your items
- Send you messages through your listings (they can still message through existing orders)
