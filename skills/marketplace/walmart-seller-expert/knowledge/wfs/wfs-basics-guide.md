# WFS (Walmart Fulfillment Services) Basics & Setup: Complete Guide

## Overview

Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS) is an end-to-end logistics solution allowing Marketplace sellers to leverage Walmart's infrastructure for storage, picking, packing, shipping, customer support, and returns processing. WFS handles everything from imports to storage to 2-day fulfillment and beyond.

### Key Value Proposition
- Sellers using WFS see **50% GMV growth on average** for items with fulfillment badges.
- WFS rates average **15% less** than most competitors.
- No signup or monthly subscription fees.
- No minimum or maximum inventory requirements.

---

## WFS Benefits

### Customer Experience & Visibility
- Fast **2-day delivery** (heavier items may take longer via ground transport).
- Enhanced search ranking through "Fulfilled by Walmart" and "2-day shipping" tags.
- Free in-store returns at **4,500+ Walmart locations** plus mail returns.
- Walmart handles all customer service for WFS orders.

### Cost Advantages
- No signup or monthly subscription fees.
- Cost-effective storage with long-term fee waiver (charges begin after 1 year, not 6 months like competitors).
- Straightforward, item-based pricing with no hidden fees.
- Free to ship and store any amount of inventory -- no minimums or maximums.

### Flexibility & Transparency
- Track inventory, orders, and shipments via accessible reports.
- Centralize inventory across multiple sales channels via Multichannel Solutions (MCS).
- Full supply chain support including freight imports and preferred carrier options.

|----------------|----------------|
| Up to 1 lb | $3.45 |
| 2 lb | $4.95 |
| 3 lb | $5.45 |
| 4-20 lb | $5.75 + $0.40 per lb over 4 lb |
| 21-30 lb | $15.55 + $0.40 per lb over 21 lb |
| 31-50 lb | $14.55 + $0.40 per lb over 31 lb |
| 51+ lb | $17.55 + $0.40 per lb over 51 lb |

### Additional Fulfillment Surcharges

| Surcharge Type | Fee |
|---------------|-----|
| Apparel | +$0.50 per item |
| Hazardous materials | +$0.50 per item |
| Retail price under $10 | +$1.00 per item |
| Oversize Tier 1 (longest side >48" to 96", median >30", or longest+girth >105" to 130") | +$3.00 |
| Oversize Tier 2 (longest side >96" to 108" or longest+girth >130" to 165") | +$20.00 |

### Big & Bulky Items

Items exceeding standard criteria (weight 150-500 lb, longest side 108-120", or longest+girth >165"):
- **Fee**: $155 + $0.80 per lb over 90 lb

### Storage Fees

**Up to 365 Days**:

| Period | Fee |
|--------|-----|
| January - September | $0.75 per cubic foot per month |
| October - December (first 30 days) | $0.75 per cubic foot per month |
| October - December (beyond 30 days) | Additional $1.50 per cubic foot per month |

**More Than 365 Days** (effective June 9, 2025):
- $2.25 per cubic foot per month

**Calculation**: (length x width x height in inches) / 1,728 = cubic feet

## Eligibility Requirements

### Product Specifications
- Maximum weight: **500 lbs** (including packaging)
- Maximum dimensions: **120" x 105" x 93"** (including packaging)
- No regulated or perishable items requiring temperature control
- Must originate from U.S. or clear customs before delivery to WFS
- Items must comply with WFS and Marketplace prohibited product policies

### Seller Requirements
- Must maintain an active Marketplace seller account
- Recommended minimum: **50 items** to start
- If approved for Marketplace, you already have access to WFS

### Ideal Items for WFS
- Best-sellers with proven demand
- Premium brands
- Items new to Walmart.com
- Products where 2-day shipping provides a competitive advantage

### Items NOT Ideal for WFS
- Slow-moving or out-of-season products
- Customizable items requiring personalization
- Items with very low sales velocity

## Onboarding Process

### Step 1: Create/Update Marketplace Account
- Existing sellers add WFS details in Seller Center.
- New sellers complete Marketplace onboarding first, then enable WFS.

### Step 2: Configure WFS Settings

**Contact Information**:
- Add WFS-specific contact details for communication about fulfillment operations.

**Billing Information**:
- Add billing method for WFS fees.
- Walmart verifies billing within **2-3 business days** before fulfillment begins.
- WFS costs are deducted from your WFS sales proceeds.

**Return Rules**:
- Configure how returns are handled: standard return processing, "Keep It" rules, or "Partial Keep It" rules.
- "Keep It" rules (honored when possible) allow customers to keep low-value items rather than returning them.

### Step 3: Add or Convert Items
- Add new items directly as WFS-fulfilled, or convert existing seller-fulfilled items.
- Additional item specifications required: country of origin, dimensions, weight, packaging details.
- Complete hazmat screening if applicable.

### Step 4: Create Shipping Plan & Send Inventory
- Create a shipping plan specifying what items you are sending and to which fulfillment center.
- Ship inventory to the assigned WFS fulfillment center(s).
- Receiving typically takes **2 business days** but may extend to **10 business days** if prep work is needed.

### Step 5: Go Live
- Once inventory is received and processed, items become available for purchase with WFS badges.

## International Sellers

- International sellers can use WFS but must clear customs before delivering inventory to WFS fulfillment centers.
- **Walmart Cross Border - Imports** program provides full/LCL ocean transportation from Asia with optional customs support.
- International sellers must comply with all U.S. product regulations and labeling requirements.
- WFS Preferred Carrier Program only accepts shipments from the 48 contiguous United States -- international sellers must use alternative inbound shipping methods.

## Item Selection Criteria

### Recommended Item Characteristics
- Strong sales history or high demand potential
- Competitive pricing that benefits from 2-day shipping visibility
- Proper product identifiers (GTIN/UPC)
- Compliant packaging and labeling
- Items where faster shipping drives conversion

### WFS Prohibited Products

WFS has specific prohibited product categories beyond the general Marketplace restrictions:

- **Hazmat items** (may be eligible with proper documentation and Safety Data Sheets)
- **Live plants** (specific restrictions apply)
- **DOT hazardous items** (regulated by Department of Transportation)
- **Pesticide products** (specific limitations)
- **Perishable items** requiring temperature control
- **Regulated items** that cannot be stored in standard warehousing
- Items that violate eCommerce Compliance team restrictions

## Converting Seller-Fulfilled Items to WFS

- Navigate to your catalog in Seller Center.
- Select items you want to convert to WFS fulfillment.
- Single or bulk conversion is available.
- Additional information required upon conversion:
  - Country of origin
  - Item dimensions and weight (with packaging)
  - Trade item configuration details
  - Hazardous material declarations (if applicable)
  - Safety Data Sheets (for hazmat items)
  - Battery information (for items containing batteries)

---

## Item Template for WFS

- WFS items require a specific item template with additional fields beyond standard Marketplace listings.
- Required fields include: dimensions, weight, packaging type, country of origin, and compliance information.
- Use the WFS-specific template available in Seller Center for bulk item setup.
- Template errors can be troubleshot through the Activity Feed.

---

## Trade Item Configuration

- Trade item configuration specifies how items are packaged and shipped.
- Details include: each unit dimensions, inner pack quantities, master case configurations.
- Accurate trade item configuration ensures proper receiving and storage at fulfillment centers.
- Incorrect configuration can lead to receiving delays or inventory rejections.

---

## Hazmat Setup

- Hazardous materials require additional setup and documentation.
- Submit **Safety Data Sheets (SDS)** for applicable items.
- Complete the hazmat declaration process in Seller Center.
- Hazmat items incur an additional **$0.50 fulfillment surcharge** per item.
- Dangerous goods and hazmat items are **prohibited on Preferred Carrier shipments** -- sellers must use their own carriers for inbound shipping.

---

## Battery-Containing Items

- Items containing batteries require specific declarations during item setup.
- Battery type, size, and quantity must be documented.
- Compliance with battery shipping regulations is mandatory.
- Specific packaging requirements apply to battery-containing items.

---

## Multi-Box Items

- Items that ship in multiple boxes can be set up as multi-box items in WFS.
- Each box in the set requires individual dimension and weight specifications.
- WFS handles the coordination of multi-box shipments to customers.
- Specific setup procedures are required through Seller Center.

---

## Big & Bulky Items

- Items exceeding standard WFS size/weight criteria qualify as Big & Bulky.
- Criteria: Weight 150-500 lb, longest side 108-120", or longest+girth >165".
- Higher fulfillment fees apply ($155 base + $0.80/lb over 90 lb).
- Specialized handling and shipping processes.
- Delivery times may be longer than standard 2-day shipping.
