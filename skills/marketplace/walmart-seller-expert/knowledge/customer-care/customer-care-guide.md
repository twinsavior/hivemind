# Walmart Marketplace Customer Care Guide

## Overview

Walmart Marketplace requires all sellers to provide high-quality customer service. Sellers must respond promptly, communicate professionally, and resolve issues efficiently. Customer care performance is tracked through the Seller Response Rate metric and directly impacts account standing.

---

## Response Time Requirements

### Standard Inquiries
- **Response deadline**: 48 hours from receipt of any customer or Walmart Customer Care inquiry
- **Applies to**: Email, phone, and Seller Center Inbox messages
- **Includes**: Weekends, Additional Days Off, and Operational Outage periods
- **Metric**: Seller Response Rate must be maintained at greater than 95%

### BBB and Attorney General Complaints
- **Response deadline**: 1 hour from receipt
- **Priority**: These are treated as urgent escalations requiring immediate attention

### Auto-Reply Policy
- Walmart does not consider auto-reply messages to be high-quality communication
- Sellers must respond personally to every customer inquiry
- Automated responses that require multiple follow-ups are prohibited

---

## Communication Standards

### Required Practices
- Provide customer support in English for U.S. transactions
- Maintain voicemail availability when a live agent is not available
- Play a greeting message before placing customers in a call queue
- Disclose call monitoring or recording to customers at the start of the call
- Present yourself as a separate entity from Walmart.com
- Supply accurate and current contact information
- Monitor spam folders and whitelist Walmart email addresses
- Proofread responses for correct grammar and spelling
- Acknowledge and empathize with customers to build trust
- Respond personally (not with automated messages)

### Prohibited Practices
- Including marketing materials or external website links in communications
- Misleading or overpromising to customers
- Initiating unsolicited phone contact outside Seller Center Inbox
- Placing advertisements in greetings or voicemail messages
- Using Google Voice or internet telephone services for customer support
- Using private residential phone numbers for customer service
- Sending unsolicited marketing communications
- Using abusive language toward customers, Walmart, or competitors
- Including "Walmart" in customer service email addresses
- Haggling to avoid returns or refunds
- Directing customers to external websites, platforms, or storefronts outside of Walmart Marketplace
- Using hyperlinks or social media references in communications or fulfillment materials

---

## Messaging Guidelines

### Seller Center Inbox
- The Seller Center Inbox is the primary communication channel with customers
- All customer-initiated messages appear here
- Sellers must monitor and respond through this channel
- Do not initiate contact via phone or any other method outside the Seller Center Inbox
- Exception: If a customer contacts you by phone using your information on the Walmart platform, you may provide assistance

### Message Quality Standards
- Do not mislead or overpromise
- Provide accurate, helpful information
- Acknowledge and empathize with the customer
- Offer clear solutions or next steps
- Avoid requiring multiple follow-ups from the customer

---

## Handling Customer Complaints

### General Approach
- Respond within the 48-hour window (1 hour for BBB/AG complaints)
- Acknowledge the issue and empathize with the customer
- Provide a clear resolution path
- Do not negotiate to avoid returns or refunds
- Do not offer discounts, alternative replacements, store credit, or incentives to avoid a return

### Order-Related Issues
- For unshipped orders: Address promptly before Walmart auto-cancels
- For shipped orders: Provide tracking information and estimated delivery
- For damaged or incorrect items: Process replacement or refund per return policy
- For missing items: Investigate and resolve within the response window

### Prohibited Resolution Tactics
- Offering discounts or incentives to avoid returns
- Haggling with customers
- Directing customers to contact you outside of Seller Center
- Providing inaccurate information about return policies

---

## Escalation Processes

### Walmart Customer Care Involvement
- Walmart Customer Care may contact sellers on behalf of customers
- Responses to Walmart Care inquiries follow the same 48-hour requirement
- Walmart may issue refunds on behalf of sellers if customer care issues are not resolved

### BBB and Attorney General Complaints
- These require a high-quality response within 1 hour
- Treat these as the highest priority escalations
- Document your response thoroughly

### Non-Response Consequences
If you fail to respond within 48 hours, Walmart may:
- Cancel unshipped orders
- Refund shipped orders
- Impose category restrictions on your account
- Suspend your account
- You cannot dispute order cancellations or refunds resulting from failure to respond within 48 hours

## Customer Satisfaction Metrics

### Seller Response Rate
- **Threshold**: Greater than 95%
- **Measurement**: Percentage of inquiries responded to within 48 hours
- **Scope**: Covers order tracking, cancellations, feedback, and return inquiries
- **Impact**: Failing to meet this standard can trigger account suppression or suspension

### Negative Feedback Rate
- **Threshold**: Less than or equal to 2% (effective spring 2026)
- **Measurement**: One-star or two-star ratings from the past 60 days
- **Drivers**: Product defects, quality concerns, incorrect item descriptions, poor customer service

### Performance Monitoring
- Review metrics regularly via the Performance dashboard in Seller Center
- Use the Walmart Seller app for on-the-go monitoring
- Leverage Seller Performance APIs for automated tracking
- Walmart sends notifications for performance issues via email and Seller Center (these communications cannot be unsubscribed from)

## Support Resources for Sellers

### Getting Help
- **Seller Center**: Click the Help button (question mark icon) in the menu bar and choose "Contact support"
- **Without Login**: Visit the Seller Center login page and select "Contact support," then choose your market and issue category
- **Support Methods**: Email, live chat, or phone depending on the situation
- **Issue Description**: Must be at least 5 words and no more than 200 words

### Additional Resources
- **Marty**: GenAI assistant for finding answers and exploring tools
- **Quick Learn**: Announcements, training videos, and walkthroughs
- **Marketplace Newsletter**: Monthly updates on news, launches, and seller information
- **Seller Academy**: Self-paced courses and video tutorials
- **Sell Better Blog**: Best practices, case studies, and seller insights

---

## Key Reminders

1. Always respond within 48 hours (1 hour for BBB/AG complaints)
2. Never use automated responses as a substitute for personal communication
3. Never direct customers to external websites or platforms
4. Do not haggle or offer incentives to avoid returns
5. Monitor your Seller Response Rate and keep it above 95%
6. Failure to meet customer care standards can result in account suspension or termination
