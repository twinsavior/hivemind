# eBay Seller Fees Complete Guide

> Complete reference for all eBay seller fees including final value fees by category, insertion fees, per-order fees, store subscriptions, promoted listings costs, international fees, and the Below Standard surcharge.

---

## Fee Structure Overview

Every eBay sale involves multiple fee components. Understanding all of them is critical for profitable pricing.

### Fee Components on a Typical Sale

| Fee Type | Typical Amount | When Charged |
|---|---|---|
| Insertion fee | $0.00 (within free allowance) or $0.35 | When listing is created/renewed |
| Final value fee (FVF) | 13.25%-15.3% of total sale amount | When item sells |
| Per-order fee | $0.30 per order | When item sells |
| Promoted Listings fee | 2%-30%+ (if used) | When item sells via ad click |
| International fee | 1.65% (if applicable) | When selling to international buyer |
| Below Standard surcharge | 6% additional (if applicable) | When item sells while Below Standard |

> **"Total sale amount"** for FVF calculation includes: item price + shipping charged to buyer + any sales tax the seller collects directly. It does NOT include sales tax collected by eBay as marketplace facilitator (which is most US states).

### Watches: Three-Tier Fee Structure

Watches have a unique tiered structure:

| Sale Price Portion | FVF Rate |
|---|---|
| $0.01 - $1,000 | 15.00% |
| $1,000.01 - $7,500 | 6.50% |
| Above $7,500 | 3.00% |

**Example**: A watch sells for $10,000
- First $1,000: $1,000 x 15.00% = $150.00
- Next $6,500: $6,500 x 6.50% = $422.50
- Last $2,500: $2,500 x 3.00% = $75.00
- Total FVF = $647.50 + $0.30 per-order fee = $647.80

## Per-Order Fee

A flat fee charged on every transaction.

| Seller Type | Per-Order Fee |
|---|---|
| **Standard sellers (no Store)** | $0.30 per order |
| **Store subscribers** | $0.30 per order |
| **Below Standard sellers** | $0.40 per order |

> **Note**: The per-order fee is charged once per order, not per item. If a buyer purchases 3 items from you in one order, you pay one $0.30 fee.

## Insertion Fees

Insertion fees are charged when you create a listing (or when a GTC listing auto-renews every 30 days).

### Free Listing Allowance

| Store Level | Free Insertion Fee Listings/Month | Insertion Fee After Allowance |
|---|---|---|
| No Store | 250 | $0.35 per listing |
| Starter Store ($4.95/mo) | 250 | $0.30 per listing |
| Basic Store ($21.95/mo) | 1,000 | $0.25 per listing |
| Premium Store ($59.95/mo) | 10,000 | $0.10 per listing |
| Anchor Store ($299.95/mo) | 25,000 | $0.05 per listing |
| Enterprise Store ($2,999.95/mo) | 100,000 | $0.05 per listing |

### Insertion Fee Details

- Free allowance resets on the 1st of each month
- Each listing variation does NOT count as a separate listing (one insertion fee per listing, regardless of variations)
- Relisting an unsold item uses another insertion fee credit
- GTC renewal every 30 days uses another insertion fee credit (if beyond free allowance)
- Listing in two categories doubles the insertion fee
- Auction reserve price: Additional fee of $5 or 7.5% of reserve (whichever is greater). Partially refunded ($5 max) if item sells

### When Insertion Fees Are NOT Charged

- First 250 (or more with Store) listings per month
- Relisting via "Sell Similar" within 30 days of listing ending (still counts against monthly allowance)
- Quantity changes on existing multi-quantity listings

## Promoted Listings Fees

### Promoted Listings Standard (Cost Per Sale)

- You set an **ad rate percentage** (minimum 2%, eBay suggests rates based on category)
- You are ONLY charged when an item sells AND the buyer clicked on your promoted listing within 30 days
- Fee = ad rate % x total sale amount (item price + shipping)
- This fee is IN ADDITION to the final value fee and per-order fee

| Category | eBay Suggested Ad Rate (typical) |
|---|---|
| Electronics | 3-5% |
| Clothing | 5-10% |
| Collectibles | 4-8% |
| Home & Garden | 5-10% |
| Sporting Goods | 4-7% |

### Promoted Listings Advanced (Cost Per Click)

- You set a **daily budget** and **cost-per-click bid**
- Charged every time a buyer clicks on your ad, whether or not they purchase
- Minimum daily budget: $1.00
- Minimum CPC: $0.01 (but competitive bids are typically $0.25-$2.00+)

### Promoted Listings Express (Auctions)

- One-time fee based on listing price
- Boosts auction visibility for a single day
- Fee: Typically $1-$5 depending on listing value

## International Fee

When you sell to a buyer located outside the United States, eBay charges an additional international fee.

| Fee | Rate | Applied To |
|---|---|---|
| International fee | 1.65% | Total sale amount (item + shipping) |

This fee applies whether you ship directly to the international buyer, use the Global Shipping Program, or use eBay International Shipping.

> **Note**: This is separate from any customs, duties, or additional shipping costs. It is an eBay fee for processing international transactions.

## Below Standard Fee Surcharge

If your seller level drops to Below Standard, eBay adds a surcharge to every sale.

| Surcharge | Amount |
|---|---|
| Additional FVF surcharge | 6% of total sale amount |
| Per-order fee increase | $0.40 (up from $0.30) |

**Example**: You sell an item for $50 + $5 shipping in a 13.25% FVF category.

| Fee Component | Above Standard | Below Standard |
|---|---|---|
| Final value fee | $7.29 (13.25% of $55) | $7.29 (13.25% of $55) |
| Below Standard surcharge | $0.00 | $3.30 (6% of $55) |
| Per-order fee | $0.30 | $0.40 |
| **Total fees** | **$7.59** | **$10.99** |

That is a 45% increase in fees — a powerful reason to maintain good seller performance.

## Top Rated Seller Fee Discount

Top Rated Sellers who qualify for **Top Rated Plus** on a specific listing receive a 10% discount on the final value fee for that listing.

### How the Discount Works

| Component | Standard FVF | With TRS Plus Discount |
|---|---|---|
| Base FVF (13.25% category) | 13.25% | 13.25% minus 10% = 11.93% |
| Base FVF (15.30% category) | 15.30% | 15.30% minus 10% = 13.77% |

> **Important**: The 10% discount applies to the FVF amount, not the FVF rate. So 13.25% with TRS Plus discount = 13.25% - (13.25% x 10%) = 11.925%, which eBay rounds to 11.93%.

### TRS Plus Eligibility Requirements (per listing)
- Account is Top Rated Seller
- Listing has same-day or 1-business-day handling time
- Listing offers 30-day or longer free returns

## How to Check Your Fees

### Current Fee Invoice
```
Seller Hub > Payments > Finances > Fee Invoice
```

### Fee Breakdown Per Transaction
```
Seller Hub > Orders > [Select order] > Order Details > Fees
```

### Monthly Fee Summary
```
Seller Hub > Payments > Finances > Reports > Transaction Report
```

### Fee Calculator (Before Listing)

When creating or revising a listing, the estimated fees are shown in the listing form before you submit. Review this carefully, especially for:
- Category-specific FVF rates
- Whether your free insertion fee allowance is exhausted
- Promoted Listings additional cost

## Fee Reduction Strategies

| Strategy | Savings | Effort |
|---|---|---|
| Maintain Top Rated Plus | 10% off FVF per qualifying listing | Medium (requires 1-day handling + 30-day free returns) |
| Subscribe to appropriate Store level | Reduced insertion fees | Low |
| Avoid Below Standard at all costs | Prevents 6% surcharge + $0.10 per-order increase | Medium |
| Use accurate categories | Avoid miscategorization into higher-fee categories | Low |
| Optimize Promoted Listings ad rate | Don't overpay on ad rate — start low and increase | Medium |
| Consolidate orders (combined shipping) | One per-order fee instead of multiple | Low |
| Sell high-value items in tiered categories | Higher-value portions of sale attract lower rate | N/A (category-dependent) |
