# Amazon Restricted Products — Comprehensive Guide

## Overview

Amazon restricts certain product categories and prohibits others entirely. "Restricted" means you need Amazon's approval before listing. "Prohibited" means you cannot sell the item under any circumstances. Understanding these distinctions is critical to maintaining your selling privileges.

---

## Types of Restrictions

### 1. Restricted (Gated) Categories

Entire product categories that require seller approval before listing. These restrictions are tied to safety, regulatory compliance, and quality concerns.

### 2. Gated Brands

Specific brand names that require authorization to sell. These restrictions are tied to intellectual property protection and counterfeit prevention.

### 3. Prohibited Products

Items that cannot be sold on Amazon under any circumstances, regardless of seller status.

### 4. Condition-Based Restrictions

Some categories restrict which conditions (New, Used, Refurbished) can be listed.

## Prohibited Products (Cannot Be Sold)

The following items are **never** allowed on Amazon:

- Illegal drugs and controlled substances.
- Firearms, ammunition, and most weapons (including assault weapons, firearm parts, and conversion kits).
- Counterfeit or pirated goods.
- Fake government-issued documents or IDs.
- Strike-anywhere matches.
- Products that promote or glorify violence, hatred, or discrimination.
- Recalled products (must be removed immediately upon recall notice).
- Products violating federal, state, or local laws.
- Human body parts or remains.
- Hazardous materials not properly classified and documented.
- Lock-picking devices (in some jurisdictions).
- Surveillance equipment marketed for illegal purposes.
- Products containing certain banned chemicals or substances.
- Items that violate intellectual property rights.

## Product Condition Guidelines

### Standard Product Conditions

| Condition | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **New** | Unused, unopened, in original factory packaging. Must include warranty details when applicable. Never been opened or used. |
| **Renewed** | Pre-owned, inspected and tested by Amazon-qualified suppliers. Minimal to no signs of wear visible from 12 inches away. Comes with 90-day warranty for replacement or refund. |
| **Rental** | Previously used items inspected and graded by qualified supplier, deemed functional and sellable. |
| **Used - Like New / Open Box** | Perfect working condition. May be missing original shrink wrap but overall intact and fully functional. Appears unopened or minimally tested. |
| **Used - Very Good** | Minor wear, good working condition. May have minor cosmetic blemishes but fully functional. |
| **Used - Good** | May show wear from consistent use, including minor cosmetic damage. Still functional. May be missing some accessories or original packaging. |
| **Used - Acceptable** | Visibly worn with damaged packaging, worn corners, scratches, and dents. Still functional. |
| **Collectible** | Items with additional value and cultural significance (e.g., autographed, vintage, out-of-print). Must detail the item's collectible significance on product page. |

### Book-Specific Conditions

| Condition | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **New** | Fresh from printer, intact cover, all pages, protective wrapping |
| **Used - Like New** | Minor marks or bends, intact dust covers |
| **Used - Very Good** | Regular wear, missing shrink wrap or dust covers allowed |
| **Used - Good** | Intact pages and covers, worn spines, limited notes acceptable |
| **Used - Acceptable** | Minor water damage acceptable if text remains readable |
| **Unacceptable** | Missing pages, obscured text, advance reading copies of in-print books |

### Collectible Book Conditions

| Condition | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Collectible - Like New** | Very small flaws, intact pages/covers, uncreased spine |
| **Collectible - Very Good** | Intact pages without notes, minor spine wear |
| **Collectible - Good** | Intact pages and covers, not generally collectible grade |
| **Collectible - Acceptable** | Readable with significant signs of wear, dust jacket may be missing |

### Category-Specific Condition Rules

- **Software & Computer Games:** Only full retail versions permitted.
- **Cell Phones & Accessories:** Specific requirements for Used and Renewed listings.
- **Personal Computers:** Hard drive must be restored to factory settings.
- **Ink and Toner Cartridges:** Used/renewed cartridges are prohibited.
- **Watches:** Pre-approval required for all conditions.
- **Consumer Electronics:** May require approval.
- **Consumables/Ingestibles/Topicals:** Strict condition and expiration requirements.

## The Ungating Process

### How to Check If a Product Is Gated

1. Go to **Seller Central > Catalog > Add Products**.
2. Search for the product by ASIN, UPC, or name.
3. If you see a **"Request Approval"** button, the product is gated.
4. If you can enter price and quantity directly, the product is ungated.

### Step-by-Step Ungating Process

**Step 1: Prepare Your Account**
- Must have a **Professional selling plan** ($39.99/month).
- Maintain good account health:
  - Order Defect Rate below **1%**.
  - Late Shipment Rate below **4%**.
  - Pre-Fulfillment Cancel Rate below **2.5%**.
- Some categories have minimum selling history requirements.

**Step 2: Gather Required Documentation**

Common documentation requirements:

| Document | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Supplier invoices** | 3+ recent invoices (within 90-180 days) from legitimate, verifiable suppliers. Must show supplier name, address, and contact info. Must list the specific products (matching ASINs). Must show minimum quantity (often 10+ units). |
| **Product images** | Photos showing all sides, labels, UPC codes, and expiration dates (if applicable). |
| **Safety certifications** | CPSC, FDA, EPA, or other regulatory certifications depending on the category. |
| **Certificates of Analysis** | Lab testing results for supplements, beauty products, etc. |
| **Certificates of Authenticity** | For branded products, collectibles, and luxury items. |
| **Business licenses** | State or local business licenses as applicable. |
| **Letters of authorization** | From brand owners (for gated brands). |

**Step 3: Submit Your Application**
1. Go to **Seller Central > Catalog > Add Products**.
2. Find the restricted item.
3. Click **"Show limitations"** or **"Request Approval."**
4. Upload your documentation.
5. Submit the application.

**Step 4: Wait for Review**
- Most approvals take **1-5 business days**.
- Some categories require additional review and may take **1-4 weeks**.
- Complex categories (supplements, medical devices) may take longer.

**Step 5: If Denied**
- Review Amazon's feedback carefully.
- Fix the specific issues identified.
- Resubmit with corrected or additional documentation.
- You can reapply; there is no limit on application attempts.

### Tips for Successful Ungating

1. **Use established, reputable suppliers** — Amazon verifies supplier legitimacy.
2. **Invoices must be professional** — letterhead, line items matching the ASIN, clear formatting.
3. **Do not use altered or fabricated documents** — Amazon cross-references supplier information.
4. **Start with easier categories** — build your account history before attempting highly restricted ones.
5. **Some categories auto-approve** — Amazon may instantly approve you for certain categories based on your account history.
6. **Consider IP Accelerator** for brand-gated products if you are the brand owner.

## FBA-Specific Restrictions

### Products Not Eligible for FBA

Certain products cannot be stored or fulfilled through Amazon's fulfillment centers:

- **Hazardous materials (hazmat)** — Products containing flammable, corrosive, or toxic substances require special hazmat review and approval. Many common products (aerosols, batteries, cleaning products) fall into this category.
- **Products with specific temperature requirements** — FBA does not offer cold storage.
- **Products requiring special handling** — Items that cannot be safely stored in a warehouse environment.
- **Recalled products** — Cannot be sent to FBA under any circumstances.
- **Expired or close-to-expiration products** — Products must have sufficient shelf life remaining.

### FBA Hazmat Review

Products flagged as potential hazmat must go through Amazon's hazmat review process:
1. Amazon identifies potential hazmat products during listing creation or inbound shipment.
2. Seller must provide a Safety Data Sheet (SDS) or product exemption sheet.
3. Amazon reviews the documentation.
4. If approved, the product can be sent to FBA.
5. If not approved, the product must be fulfilled through FBM.

### Meltable Products

Temperature-sensitive products (chocolate, candles, certain cosmetics) may be subject to seasonal FBA restrictions. Amazon may limit inbound shipments during summer months to prevent damage.

### Expiration Date Requirements

Products with expiration dates must:
- Have dates clearly printed on the packaging.
- Arrive at the fulfillment center with sufficient remaining shelf life (typically 90+ days).
- Be removed or disposed of before expiration at the seller's expense.

## Consequences of Non-Compliance

Selling restricted or prohibited products without proper approval can result in:

- **Listing removal** — Individual listings taken down.
- **Inventory destruction** — Amazon may destroy non-compliant inventory in FBA.
- **Funds withheld** — Payments may be held pending investigation.
- **Account suspension** — Temporary loss of selling privileges.
- **Permanent ban** — Repeat or serious violations can result in permanent account deactivation.
- **Legal action** — For prohibited or illegal products.

---

## How Restrictions Change Over Time

- Categories that were open may become gated at any time.
- Previously gated categories may loosen restrictions.
- Brand gating can be requested by brand owners through Brand Registry.
- Seasonal restrictions (e.g., Toys & Games during holiday season) activate and deactivate annually.
- **Always check current restrictions** in Seller Central before listing new products.
