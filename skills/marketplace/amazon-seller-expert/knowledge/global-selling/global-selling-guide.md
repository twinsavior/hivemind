# Amazon Global Selling — Comprehensive Guide

## Overview

Amazon Global Selling allows you to list and sell products on Amazon marketplaces around the world. With a single Professional selling account as your foundation, you can expand to multiple countries, leveraging Amazon's fulfillment infrastructure, logistics tools, and customer base in each region.

## Account Structure

### Unified Accounts

- **North America and Brazil unified account** — A single account covers US, Canada, Mexico, and Brazil. Your existing US seller account can be extended.
- **European unified account** — One account for all European marketplaces.
- **Country-specific accounts** — Required for Japan, India, Australia, Singapore, UAE, Saudi Arabia, and Egypt.

### Pricing

When linking global accounts, you pay **either the equivalent of USD $39.99/month** or the sum of the selling plan fees for each region, **whichever is lower**. This means expanding internationally does not necessarily double or triple your subscription costs.

## How to Expand Internationally

### Six-Step Process

1. **Create accounts** for target regions/countries.
2. **Select products** — Review local demand using Marketplace Product Guidance, Product Opportunity Explorer, and Best Sellers lists.
3. **Complete requirements** — Taxes, regulatory compliance, product certifications for each target country.
4. **List products** — Use Build International Listings tool for translation and price synchronization.
5. **Ship and fulfill** — Choose FBA, FBM, or hybrid fulfillment.
6. **Manage operations** — Use Amazon's tools for multi-country management.

### Product Selection Tools

| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Marketplace Product Guidance** | Explores global demand for your existing products |
| **Sell Globally Dashboard** | Shows sales potential by country with trend insights |
| **Product Opportunity Explorer** | Identifies customer demand patterns in each marketplace |
| **Best Sellers Lists** | Available for each Amazon store |

## Fulfillment Options for International Selling

### Remote Fulfillment with FBA

**What it is:** Use your existing US FBA inventory to fulfill orders in Canada, Mexico, and Brazil without shipping inventory to those countries.

**How it works:**
- Create offers on Amazon.ca, Amazon.com.mx, and Amazon.com.br.
- Amazon handles export logistics, fulfillment, customer inquiries, and returns.
- The Build International Listings tool automatically replicates eligible US offers and adjusts pricing for fulfillment costs, referral fees, and currency exchange rates.

**Requirements:**
- Professional selling plan.
- North America and Brazil unified selling account.
- FBA enrollment in the US with FBA Export enabled.

**Fees:** Standard Remote Fulfillment with FBA costs plus applicable category referral fee for the destination store (replaces US FBA costs).

**Tax handling:** Customers pay any import duties, taxes, and fees as the importer of record — sellers avoid international tax obligations.

**Returns:** Amazon manages returns following standard FBA policies; items ship back to the US.

**Eligible countries:** Canada, Mexico, Brazil.

**Not eligible:** Restricted, prohibited, and dangerous goods.

### FBA Export

**What it is:** Enables international customers on amazon.com to purchase your FBA products with international shipping included.

**How it works:**
- Customers see a checkout price including international shipping and import fees.
- Amazon handles fulfillment at no extra cost to the seller.
- Most FBA sellers are **enrolled automatically**.

**Verification:** Check or modify your FBA Export status in Seller Central > Fulfillment by Amazon Settings > Export Settings.

### Pan-European FBA

**What it is:** Amazon distributes your inventory across fulfillment centers in France, Germany, Italy, Spain, and Poland based on anticipated demand.

**Key benefits:**
- Up to **53% savings** in fulfillment fees compared to local fulfillment.
- Automatic inventory distribution at no additional cost.
- Products remain Prime-eligible across all participating nations.

**Requirements:**
- List products using identical SKUs across France, Germany, Italy, and Spain.
- Enable at least two countries for FBA storage.
- Register for VAT in each participating country.
- Export inventory to a fulfillment center in an enabled country.

**Important:** The UK exited Pan-European FBA effective January 1, 2021. UK sales require separate inventory export to UK fulfillment centers.

### Multi-Channel Fulfillment (MCF)

**What it is:** Use FBA inventory to fulfill orders from your own website, Shopify, WooCommerce, and other non-Amazon sales channels.

**Delivery speeds:**
- **Expedited:** 2 business days (click-to-delivery).
- **Standard:** 3 business days (click-to-delivery).

**Integrations:** Shopify, WooCommerce, 100+ prebuilt integrations, custom API.

**New seller incentive:** 25% discount on first 100 units shipped with MCF plus additional credits.

**Availability:** US, Canada, Mexico, UK, Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Japan, and Australia.

## Supply Chain by Amazon

### What It Is

A fully automated, end-to-end supply chain solution that moves products from manufacturers to customers worldwide. It integrates six Amazon services into one unified platform.

### Included Services

| Service | Function |
|---------|----------|
| **Amazon Global Logistics (AGL)** | International freight shipping from China/Vietnam |
| **Seller Export & Delivery (SEND)** | Cross-border export between 14 countries |
| **Partner Carrier Program (PCP)** | Domestic US transportation (up to 25% savings) |
| **Amazon Warehousing & Distribution (AWD)** | Bulk storage and inventory management |
| **Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA)** | Order picking, packing, and delivery |
| **Multi-Channel Fulfillment (MCF)** | Third-party marketplace fulfillment |

### How It Works

1. **Inbound logistics** — Products ship from manufacturers via AGL, SEND, or PCP to AWD facilities.
2. **Storage & distribution** — Inventory stored cost-effectively at AWD, buyable the moment it is received.
3. **Automated replenishment** — Algorithm monitors FBA inventory levels and automatically triggers replenishment.
4. **Order fulfillment** — Orders picked, packed, and delivered through FBA, MCF, or Buy with Prime.

### Cost Savings

- **38% storage cost reduction** vs. FBA during non-holiday months.
- **80% storage reduction** during holidays.
- **20% AWD storage discount** when using Amazon Managed Service.
- **10% transportation cost savings**.
- FBA inbound placement costs, storage-utilization surcharges, and low-inventory-level penalties **waived** for AWD shipments.
- FBA storage surcharges **waived entirely** when auto-replenishment is enabled.

### AWD Auto-Replenishment

Drives a **15% increase in unit sales on average** by enabling same-day and next-day delivery options through optimal inventory positioning.

## Amazon Global Logistics (AGL)

### What It Is

A door-to-door freight service shipping inventory from China and Vietnam directly to Amazon fulfillment centers.

### Shipping Lanes

**Ship from:** Mainland China, Hong Kong, Vietnam.

**Ship to:** US, UK, France, Germany, Italy, Spain, Japan.

### Transportation Options

| Option | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Standard Ocean Freight** | FCL and LCL options with floor loading and pallet shipping |
| **Fast Ocean Freight** | Expedited ocean service; faster than standard, cheaper than air |
| **Air Freight** | Goods delivered to fulfillment centers within 7-10 days |

### Inventory Placement Options

| Option | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Amazon Managed Placement** | Amazon optimizes distribution across fulfillment centers; no separate FBA inbound placement fees |
| **Seller Managed Placement** | Direct shipment to regional facilities; requires min. 5 identical boxes per item |
| **Minimal Shipment Splits** | Single-facility delivery for large/bulky items; standard FBA inbound placement fees apply |

### Included Services

- Local cargo pickup at origin.
- Export documentation and declarations.
- International ocean and air freight.
- Customs clearance at origin and destination.
- Drayage services.
- Optional palletization and labeling.
- Cargo insurance available.

### New Seller Incentive

**8% discount** on base ocean freight rates for 60 days following profile creation.

## SEND Program (Seller Export & Delivery)

### What It Is

An end-to-end shipping solution for moving inventory globally by air or ocean between 14 countries, integrated with FBA and AWD.

### Coverage

**Ship from:** India, China, Korea, Taiwan, Thailand, Vietnam, Germany, Mexico, UK, US.

**Ship to:** US, UK, Germany, Japan, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Australia.

### Key Benefits

- Competitive carrier rates.
- Book shipments, schedule pickups, pay charges, and track all in Seller Central.
- Carriers provide door-to-door service.
- Carrier handles both origin and destination customs clearance.
- No separate carrier accounts or credit checks required.

### Costs

- **Freight charges:** Posted within 24 hours of booking as "FBA international shipping charge."
- **Customs duties:** Charged after delivery based on actual amounts; appears as "FBA international shipping customs charge."
- All charges billed in USD; visible in Seller Central Payments report.

### How to Start

1. Log into Professional Seller Central account.
2. Use the Send to Amazon workflow; select SEND in step 2.
3. Track shipments via Shipping Queue (FBA) or AWD page.

## Language & Localization

### Translation Tools

- **Build International Listings** tool simplifies multi-marketplace listing creation.
- Matching existing products in a marketplace often does not require translation.
- New listings are frequently auto-translated by Amazon; paid professional translation services are also available.

### Customer Service Languages

- Customer service must be provided in the local language of each marketplace.
- FBA handles customer service automatically in the local language.
- FBM sellers may need to hire local support providers or use Customer Service by Amazon.
- Amazon's Service Provider Network offers vetted translation and customer service providers.

## Tax & Regulatory Considerations

### Key Points

- **Product requirements vary by country** — review specific obligations for each market.
- **Manage Your Compliance** tool in Seller Central helps track requirements.
- **Compliance Reference** tool provides product standard information.
- Consult tax and regulatory experts for each target country.
- VAT registration is required in European countries where you store inventory (Pan-European FBA).
- Some countries require specific product certifications, labeling, or testing.

### Currency & Payments

- **Amazon Currency Converter** deposits international sales proceeds to your US bank account in USD.
- Alternatively, establish local bank accounts in each country (may require local business entity).
- Exchange rates are applied at time of disbursement.

## Key Tips for International Expansion

1. **Start with Remote Fulfillment** to test Canada, Mexico, and Brazil with zero additional inventory.
2. **Use FBA Export** to test international demand on amazon.com before committing to local inventory.
3. **Pan-European FBA** is the most cost-effective way to serve European customers.
4. **Research local regulations** thoroughly — product compliance varies dramatically between countries.
5. **Leverage Supply Chain by Amazon** for end-to-end logistics management.
6. **Use Amazon Global Logistics** if sourcing from China or Vietnam.
7. **Monitor currency exchange rates** — they directly impact your margins.
8. **New sellers** using the New Seller Guide generate approximately **6x more first-year sales on average** during their first 90 days.
