# Seller Hub — Complete Navigation and Analytics Guide

## Overview

Seller Hub is eBay's centralized dashboard for managing your selling business. It consolidates orders, listings, marketing, performance metrics, research tools, and payments into a single interface.

**Access:** Log in to eBay > click your username (top left) > select "Seller Hub," or navigate directly to `ebay.com/sh/ovw`.

**Opt-in:** Seller Hub is free. If not already enabled, go to `ebay.com/sellerhub` and click "Start using Seller Hub."

---

## Dashboard (Overview Tab)

**Navigation:** Seller Hub > Overview

The Overview page is your daily command center. It displays:

### Summary Cards

| Card | What It Shows |
|---|---|
| Sales | Total sales for today, 7 days, 31 days, and custom range |
| Orders | Orders awaiting shipment, shipped, returned, cancelled |
| Listings | Active, sold, unsold, draft counts |
| Selling costs | Fees, shipping labels, promoted listings costs |
| Impressions | Total listing impressions over the selected period |
| Payouts | Pending and completed payout amounts |

### Tasks and Alerts

- Items requiring action (ship by date approaching, return requests, cases opened).
- Listing violations or policy warnings.
- Seller level notifications (approaching Below Standard, etc.).
- Promotional opportunities and seasonal tips.

### Monthly Limits

Displays your current selling allowance (number of items and total dollar amount) and usage against those limits.

## Orders Tab

**Navigation:** Seller Hub > Orders

### Sub-tabs

| Sub-tab | Purpose |
|---|---|
| All orders | Complete order list with filters |
| Awaiting shipment | Orders that need to be shipped (most frequently used) |
| Awaiting payment | Unpaid orders (rare with Managed Payments) |
| Paid and shipped | Completed orders with tracking uploaded |
| Cancellations | Orders cancelled by buyer or seller |
| Returns | Active return requests |

### Key Actions from the Orders Tab

1. **Print shipping labels:** Select orders > Actions > Print shipping label.
2. **Upload tracking:** Select order > Add tracking number > Enter carrier and tracking ID.
3. **Bulk actions:** Select multiple orders for bulk shipping label printing or bulk tracking upload.
4. **Send invoice:** For best offer or multi-item orders.
5. **Cancel order:** Select order > Cancel this order > Choose reason.

### Order Filters

- Date range (custom, 7 days, 30 days, 60 days, 90 days).
- Buyer username.
- Item title or item number.
- Order status.
- Ship-to country.

### Downloading Order Data

**Navigation:** Orders > All orders > Download report (CSV icon, top right)

Exports: Order number, buyer ID, item title, item number, sale price, shipping cost, order date, payment date, ship-to address, tracking number.

## Listings Tab

**Navigation:** Seller Hub > Listings

### Sub-tabs

| Sub-tab | What It Contains |
|---|---|
| Active | All currently live listings |
| Unsold | Ended listings that did not sell (available for relist) |
| Drafts | Saved but not yet published listings |
| Scheduled | Listings set to go live at a future date |
| Ended | All ended listings (sold and unsold) |
| Selling offers | Offers sent to watchers or best offer responses |

### Active Listings Management

Each listing row shows:
- Thumbnail, title, item number.
- Current price, quantity available, quantity sold.
- Watchers count.
- Views (last 30 days).
- Listing format (auction, fixed price).
- Time left or GTC renewal date.
- Promoted status (ad rate percentage if promoted).

### Bulk Listing Actions

Select multiple listings, then use the bulk action dropdown:

| Action | What It Does |
|---|---|
| Edit | Bulk edit price, quantity, or item specifics |
| Relist | Relist ended/unsold items |
| End listings | End active listings |
| Move to drafts | Save listings as drafts |
| Add to Promoted | Add listings to a Promoted Listings campaign |
| Revise shipping | Bulk change shipping options or handling time |
| Download | Export listing data as CSV |

### Listing Quality Report

**Navigation:** Seller Hub > Listings > Listing quality report (link at top of Active listings)

This report identifies listings with quality issues that hurt search visibility:

| Issue Type | Impact | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| Missing item specifics | Lower search ranking | Add all recommended and required item specifics |
| Poor photo quality | Lower click-through rate | Use 1600x1600px minimum, white background, multiple angles |
| Title optimization | Reduced keyword matching | Include brand, model, size, color, condition in title |
| Incorrect category | Listing may not appear in relevant searches | Recategorize to the most specific applicable category |
| Missing condition description | Buyer uncertainty, potential returns | Add accurate condition description for used items |
| No returns accepted | Lower search ranking and buyer trust | Offer at least 30-day returns |

## Performance Tab

**Navigation:** Seller Hub > Performance

This is where you monitor your seller health and business analytics.

### Service Metrics

**Navigation:** Performance > Service metrics

Displays your current seller level evaluation:

| Metric | Top Rated | Above Standard | Below Standard |
|---|---|---|---|
| Transaction defect rate | <=0.5% | <=2% | >2% |
| Cases closed without resolution | <=0.3% | <=0.3% | >0.3% |
| Late shipment rate | <=3% | <=7% | >7% |
| Tracking uploaded on time and validated | >=95% | — | — |

Each metric shows:
- Current rate.
- Number of transactions in the evaluation period.
- Number of defects/late shipments.
- Trend arrow (improving or worsening).
- Click into each metric for a detailed transaction-level breakdown.

### Traffic Report

**Navigation:** Performance > Traffic

The traffic report is one of the most valuable analytics tools on eBay.

#### Key Metrics Explained

| Metric | Definition | What It Tells You |
|---|---|---|
| Impressions | Number of times your listing appeared in search results or browse pages | How visible your listings are |
| Page views | Number of times a buyer viewed your listing page | How many people clicked through |
| Click-through rate | Page views / Impressions | How compelling your listing is in search results |
| Sales conversion rate | Transactions / Page views | How well your listing page converts visitors |
| Quantity sold | Number of items sold | Sales volume |
| Sales revenue | Total revenue for the period | Revenue performance |

#### How to Read the Traffic Report

1. **Set date range:** Compare week-over-week or month-over-month.
2. **Filter by listing:** Find specific items to analyze.
3. **Sort by impressions:** Identify your most visible listings.
4. **Look for mismatches:**
   - High impressions + low page views = poor title/photo/price (fix listing presentation).
   - High page views + low sales = poor listing content or pricing (fix description, condition, price).
   - Low impressions = poor SEO or low demand (improve title keywords, item specifics).

#### Traffic Report Download

Download as CSV for spreadsheet analysis. Includes item-level data for all active listings.

### Sales Report

**Navigation:** Performance > Sales

| Report Type | Content |
|---|---|
| Sales summary | Total sales, average sale price, sales count by period |
| Sales by category | Breakdown by eBay category |
| Sales by format | Auction vs. fixed price performance |
| Sales by listing type | Single vs. multi-quantity |
| Ending day/time | Which days and times generate most sales |

### Seller Level Details

**Navigation:** Performance > Seller level

Shows your current seller level and the path to the next level:

| Level | Benefits |
|---|---|
| Below Standard | Higher fees (+4% FVF surcharge), lower search visibility, selling restrictions |
| Above Standard | Standard fees, standard search visibility |
| Top Rated | 10% FVF discount on qualifying listings, Top Rated Plus badge, priority search placement |
| Top Rated Plus | Requires 1-day handling + 30-day free returns; earns TRS badge on individual listings |

Evaluation happens on the **20th of each month**. Your level is based on the previous 12 months of transactions (or 3 months for newer sellers).

## Research Tab (Terapeak)

**Navigation:** Seller Hub > Research

Terapeak is eBay's product research tool, now integrated into Seller Hub.

### Available to:

| Feature | Free Sellers | Basic Store | Premium+ Store |
|---|---|---|---|
| Product research (last 90 days) | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Sourcing insights | No | Yes | Yes |
| Extended data (up to 365 days) | No | No | Yes |

### Product Research

**Navigation:** Research > Product research

1. Enter a keyword or item number.
2. View:
   - **Average sold price** over the period.
   - **Sell-through rate** (percentage of listings that resulted in a sale).
   - **Total sales volume** and revenue.
   - **Active listing count** (current competition).
   - **Price distribution** (histogram of sale prices).
   - **Condition breakdown** (new vs. used sales).
   - **Top selling listings** (see what successful sellers are doing).

### Sourcing Insights

**Navigation:** Research > Sourcing insights

Shows trending products, supply/demand ratios, and restocking recommendations based on your selling history.

**Navigation:** Seller Hub > Payments

| Sub-tab | Content |
|---|---|

| Field | Meaning |
|---|---|

| Processing | Payments being processed (typically 1–2 business days) |
| On hold | Funds held due to tracking not uploaded, new seller hold, or dispute |

## Tips for Using Seller Hub Effectively

### Daily Routine

1. Check **Overview** for tasks and alerts.
2. Go to **Orders > Awaiting shipment** and process all orders.
3. Review any **return requests** or **cases**.
4. Check **Payments > Summary** for payout status.

### Weekly Routine

1. Review **Traffic report** for listing performance trends.
2. Check **Listing quality report** and fix flagged issues.
3. Adjust **Promoted Listings** campaigns based on 7-day performance.
4. Review **Service metrics** for any defect trend changes.

### Monthly Routine

1. Download **Sales report** and **Traffic report** for analysis.
2. Compare month-over-month metrics.
3. Review **seller level** status (especially before the 20th evaluation).
4. Check **Terapeak** for new product opportunities.
5. Plan next month's promotions and markdown events.
