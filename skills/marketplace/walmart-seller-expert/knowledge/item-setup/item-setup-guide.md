# Item Setup & Listings on Walmart Marketplace: Complete Guide

## Overview

Once you have completed onboarding in Seller Center, you can start building your product catalog on Walmart Marketplace. Walmart offers multiple setup methods to accommodate different inventory sizes, product types, and existing catalog situations. Choosing the right method depends on whether your products already exist in Walmart's catalog and how many items you need to list.

---

## Item Setup Methods

### 1. Express Setup
- **Best for**: Sellers with up to 50 items already in Walmart's catalog.
- **Description**: The fastest way to list up to 50 items at once that already exist in Walmart's catalog.
- **How it works**: Search for products in Walmart's existing catalog and quickly add your offers (price, inventory, shipping).
- **Key benefit**: Minimal data entry required; ideal for rapid catalog expansion with established products.

### 2. Match Walmart's Catalog (Setup by Match)
- **Best for**: Large-scale sellers with thousands of items already in Walmart's catalog.
- **Description**: The fastest way to list thousands of items at once that already exist in Walmart's catalog.
- **How it works**: Download Walmart's Excel template and fill out the required offer attribute information to match your items to existing items on Walmart.com.
- **Key benefit**: Efficient bulk matching for sellers with extensive existing catalogs.

### 3. Full Setup (Single Item)
- **Best for**: Individual new product listings not currently on Walmart.com.
- **Description**: List a new item not sold in Walmart's catalog with comprehensive product information.
- **How it works**: Manual, detailed entry process through Seller Center for one product at a time.
- **Key benefit**: Complete control over all product attributes for unique or specialty items.

### 4. Full Setup (Bulk/Template)
- **Best for**: Creating 50 or more new items or variant groups not in Walmart's catalog.
- **Description**: Use Walmart's Full Setup template to list new items not sold in Walmart's catalog.
- **How it works**: Download the Excel template and input required offer and product attribute information. Upload completed template to Seller Center.
- **Key benefit**: Supports multiple items simultaneously with comprehensive product information.

### 5. Import Your Catalog
- **Best for**: Sellers migrating from other marketplaces (Amazon, eBay, Shopify, etc.).
- **Description**: Import items from another external marketplace or from a Supplier One catalog.
- **How it works**: Connect your other marketplace account or upload your existing catalog data.
- **Key benefit**: Streamlines cross-platform selling; maintains existing product data.

### 6. Upload a Spreadsheet
- **Best for**: Sellers with bulk listings from external sources.
- **Description**: Upload a bulk listing report or custom spreadsheet from any external marketplace.
- **How it works**: Upload spreadsheets in any Excel format (must not exceed 5MB). Track status via the Activity Feed in Seller Center.
- **Key benefit**: Flexible data format acceptance for large dataset transfers.

### 7. Virtual Packs
- **Best for**: Sellers offering bulk discounts or multi-packs.
- **Description**: Create multiple pack sizes of an existing item with a discount for shoppers who buy in bulk.
- **How it works**: Leverages existing single-item inventory to create multi-pack SKUs.
- **Key benefit**: Encourages larger purchases without separate inventory.

### 8. Import Supplier Catalog
- **Best for**: Walmart Drop Ship Vendors or Warehouse suppliers transitioning to Marketplace.
- **Description**: Import your supplier catalog directly into your Marketplace account.
- **How it works**: If you have recently moved over from Walmart's supplier programs, you can migrate your existing catalog.
- **Key benefit**: Preserves supplier relationships and existing product hierarchies.

### 9. API Integration
- **Best for**: Sellers with large catalogs and a technical team.
- **Description**: Use Walmart's APIs to publish and manage items programmatically.
- **Key benefit**: Automation and scalability for high-volume sellers.

### 10. Solution Providers
- **Best for**: Sellers who want third-party help managing their catalog.
- **Description**: A third-party Approved Solution Provider can help you import your catalog.
- **How it works**: Connect with providers through Walmart's App Store.
- **Key benefit**: Professional catalog management support without DIY work.

### Selection Decision Matrix

| Scenario | Recommended Method |
|----------|-------------------|
| Existing Walmart products, small catalog (up to 50) | Express Setup |
| Existing Walmart products, large catalog (thousands) | Match Walmart's Catalog |
| Brand new products, individual items | Single Item Full Setup |
| Brand new products, 50+ items | Full Setup Template (Bulk) |
| Migration from another marketplace | Import Your Catalog |
| Bulk data from external sources | Upload a Spreadsheet |
| Multiple pack variations | Virtual Packs |
| Supplier transitioning to Marketplace | Import Supplier Catalog |
| Large catalog, technical team available | API Integration |

## Product Details Policy

### Content Definition
Walmart defines content as any information providing product details about an item: product name, site description, key features, and images. Content also includes item attributes -- the values helping customers understand an item's features and characteristics.

### Content Ownership
You may not always be able to define which content is displayed on Walmart.com, as Walmart determines the "best available content" unless provided by brand owners or authorized resellers. Items unable to be updated appear locked during modification attempts.

---

## Title Requirements

### Allowed
- Brief titles not exceeding **150 characters**
- Clear, descriptive language
- Proper capitalization
- Relevant attribute values (dimensions, quantities, measurements)

### Prohibited
- All-caps text or special characters (~, !, *, $)
- Promotional language (free shipping, hot sale, top-rated, clearance, Black Friday)
- Retailer names unless officially licensed or part of brand identity
- Irrelevant information (coming soon, discontinued status)
- URLs in any format
- Non-English languages (except brand or item names)
- Years unless specifically recommended by content standards
- Excessive keyword repetition

---

## Description Guidelines

### Requirements
- Minimum **150 words** (varies by product category)
- Single paragraph format
- Feature and benefit descriptions including product name and brand
- Search-relevant related terminology

### Prohibited
- Competitor exclusivity claims (Amazon exclusive, Target.com exclusive)
- Retailer names without proper licensing
- Promotional assertions
- Authenticity claims without Walmart approval (except food products)
- External URLs, emojis, special characters
- Bullet points, numbered lists, or HTML formatting
- Non-English languages
- Content that does not match the product in the title
- Excessive keyword repetition

---

## Key Features Standards

### Allowed
- Three to ten most important benefits/features listed first
- Short phrases minimizing keyword repetition

### Prohibited
- Content exceeding **80 characters per feature**
- Competitor exclusivity statements
- Promotional claims
- Irrelevant information
- External URLs, emojis, special characters
- Non-English languages
- Product misrepresentation
- Repetitive keywords

---

## Item Attribution & Categorization

- Proper categorization affects how your items appear in search and browse results on Walmart.com and Google.
- Fill in as many attributes as possible if accurate information is available -- this optimizes discoverability.
- Values must match their corresponding attributes and measurement units.
- Numeric values require actual numbers (not text); text fields should contain appropriate language.
- Category-specific guidelines apply based on product type.

---

## Keyword Optimization

- Use relevant, search-friendly keywords in product titles, descriptions, and key features.
- Avoid excessive keyword repetition or "keyword stuffing."
- Keywords should accurately reflect the product being sold.
- Think about how shoppers search for your products on Walmart.com.
- Leverage Walmart's Search Insights tools (available in Seller Center) to identify high-performing keywords.

## Image Guidelines & Requirements

### Technical Specifications

| Specification | Requirement |
|--------------|-------------|
| Supported formats | JPEG, JPG, PNG, BMP |
| Prohibited format | GIF |
| Color format | RGB only |
| File size | 5MB or less |
| Bit depth | 8 bits per pixel |
| Recommended dimensions | 2200px x 2200px |
| Minimum zoom dimensions | 1500px x 1500px |
| Swatch dimensions | 100px x 100px |
| Aspect ratio | 1:1 (Square) |
| Background | Seamless white (255/255/255 RGB) |

### File Naming & Hosting
- Recommended file naming uses the 14-digit GTIN.
- Walmart provides image hosting service -- no external hosting needed.
- File names should end in valid image extensions (.jpg, .png, etc.).
- Supported URL sources: Publicly accessible URLs from Amazon S3, Walmart Image CDN, and similar (ports 8080, 80, 443, or 8443).
- Unsupported: HTML pages, URLs with queries, Dropbox links, non-standard ports, unencoded special characters.

### Image Content Requirements
- Minimum **four images** recommended per listing.
- Must use actual product images -- stock photos are not allowed.
- Main image: professional lighting, sharp focus, white background, crop closely.
- No watermarks, seller names, or logos on main images.
- All images must correspond with product name and key attributes.
- No image duplication on the product page.
- Swatch images provided where applicable.
- Larger items may show expected environment instead of white background.

### Prohibited Image Content
- Content promoting hatred, violence, or discrimination
- Explicit nudity or vulgar language
- Obscene, sexually suggestive, or pornographic material
- Watermarks, seller names, or logos on main images
- Promotional language or product condition descriptions
- Walmart or competitor retailer names/logos
- Non-English language text
- Out-of-stock or sold-out indicators
- Accessories not included with the product

### Pre-owned / Remanufactured Items
- Images must reflect the actual condition (Like New, Good, Fair, New with Defects).
- Placeholder images are available temporarily until replaced with actual product images.

## Variant Management

### Product Variant Groups
- Variant groups let you group related items (e.g., different sizes, colors) under one product page.
- Sellers can create variant groups in Seller Center or using Walmart APIs.
- Using Bulk Upload and the Full Item Spec template, sellers can create new items and variant groups simultaneously.

### Creating Variant Groups
- **Via Seller Center Items Dashboard**: Create groups directly in the UI.
- **Via Full Setup Template**: Use the template to create 50 or more item variant groups in bulk.
- To combine variant groups: Open the template, locate the groups, and set the same variant group ID for all items.
- Changes can take up to 4 hours to appear on Walmart.com.

### Managing Variant Groups
- Add or remove variants from existing groups.
- Update variant attributes (size, color, etc.).
- Troubleshoot variant errors through the Activity Feed.

## Item Conditions (Resold Program)

### Supported Condition Types
- **New**: Brand new, unused, unopened items.
- **Refurbished / Restored**: Items professionally restored to working condition.
- **Pre-Owned / Used**: Previously owned items in various conditions.
- **Open Box**: Items removed from original packaging but unused.

### Resold Program Requirements
- Specific participation requirements apply for listing resold items.
- Images must reflect actual item condition.
- Resold items become Buy Box ineligible when priced above corresponding new items on the same product page.

---

## Automotive Fitment

- Automotive parts require industry standard fitment files.
- Must submit ACES (Aftermarket Catalog Exchange Standard) and PIES (Product Information Exchange Standard) records.
- Fitment data enables accurate year/make/model/engine matching for customers.
- Troubleshooting resources available for fitment errors.

---

## Converting Items to WFS (Walmart Fulfillment Services)

- If you have already set up items as seller-fulfilled, you can convert them to Walmart-fulfilled.
- Single or bulk conversion is available.
- Additional compliance data is required: country of origin, dimensions, weight, hazmat screening.
- Items must meet WFS trade item configuration requirements.

---

## Troubleshooting Setup Errors

- After uploading templates, track status from the **Activity Feed** in Seller Center.
- Common issues: missing required fields, invalid UPC/GTIN, category mismatches, image URL errors.
- Items appear as "Draft" until all required information is provided and account setup is complete.
- Processing note: Template uploads may take up to 4 hours to finalize.
- Spreadsheets must not exceed 5MB.
- SKU updates can appear in as quickly as 15 minutes or take up to 4 hours.

---

## GTIN Exemptions (Private Label)

- If your products do not have standard product IDs (private label, handmade items), you can request a GTIN exemption.
- Apply through Seller Center during catalog setup.
