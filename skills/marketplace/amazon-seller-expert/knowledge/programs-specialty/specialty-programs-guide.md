# Amazon Specialty Programs — Comprehensive Guide

## Overview

Amazon offers several specialty programs designed for specific seller types, product categories, and business models. This guide covers Handmade, Renewed, Custom, Buy with Prime, Subscribe & Save, and Vine.

---

## Amazon Handmade

### What It Is

A specialized marketplace for artisans to sell handcrafted goods to Amazon's global customer base. Products appear in applicable Amazon departments and the dedicated Amazon Custom store.

### Eligibility

- Individual makers and collaborative groups (cooperatives, nonprofits, charities).
- Must undergo an application and approval process.
- Existing Amazon sellers can apply without creating a new account.

### Product Qualifications

Products must fall into one of five categories:

| Type | Definition | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| **Hand-altered** | Modified premade items | Graphic-printed shirts, personalized mugs |
| **Hand-designed** | Original designs produced | Art prints, stationery |
| **Handcrafted** | Raw materials shaped manually | Carved wooden bowls, knitted scarves |
| **Repurposed** | Items transformed to new purposes | Tire planters, bottle lamps |
| **Upcycled** | Existing items improved in quality | Refinished furniture |

### Available Categories

13 categories: Accessories, Artwork, Baby, Beauty & Personal Care, Clothing/Shoes/Handbags, Home, Outdoor & Home Care, Jewelry & Watches, Kitchen & Dining, Pet Supplies, Sporting Goods, Stationery & Party Supplies, Toys & Games.

### Fee Structure

| Fee | Amount |
|-----|--------|
| **Monthly subscription** | Waived after first month (saves $480/year) |
| **Referral fee** | 15% per unit or $0.30, whichever is greater |
| **Storefront creation** | Free |

You are only charged on actual sales.

### Application Process

1. Register as a Professional seller ($39.99/month) or upgrade from Individual.
2. Complete application detailing your background, products, and creative processes.
3. List products with Handmade Classification attribute; specify type (hand-altered, hand-designed, etc.).
4. Create a maker profile describing your inspiration and process.
5. Optionally expand internationally (separate account per region required).

Check application status using email and confirmation code through the self-service portal.

### Key Features

- **Maker profile** — Showcases your story and creative process to build customer connection.
- **FBA eligible** — Use Fulfillment by Amazon for order fulfillment.
- **Amazon Custom integration** — Offer personalized products (requires FBM fulfillment).
- **International expansion** — Handmade approval transfers across regions.
- **Community** — Access the Handmade Hub and Seller Forums community group.

## Amazon Renewed

### What It Is

A program for selling refurbished electronics and other products. Sellers inspect, repair, and resell pre-owned items with a quality guarantee.

### Eligible Product Categories

Electrical or mechanical products requiring component replacement/upgrade and quality assurance:
- Phones, cameras, headphones.
- Kitchen appliances, power tools.
- Personal computers, tablets.
- Televisions, video game consoles.

### Seller Types Accepted

- Distributors/resellers.
- Manufacturers/brand owners.
- Brand-authorized refurbishers.
- Independent refurbishers.
- Repairers.
- IT Asset Disposition (ITAD) providers.

### Qualification Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Purchase invoices** | Itemized invoices showing minimum **$50,000** in refurbished purchases (previous 90-180 days) |
| **Apple products** | **$2.5 million** threshold for Apple-branded items |
| **Trademark documentation** | Or Brand Registry enrollment |
| **Sample images** | Product and packaging photos demonstrating "Excellent" cosmetic condition |

### Application Process

1. Become a registered Amazon seller.
2. Complete the Amazon Renewed application.
3. Approval typically within **10 days** (variable).
4. List products using the Certified Refurbished ASIN Creation tool.
5. Price at minimum **5% discount** versus new counterparts.
6. Fulfill orders and maintain quality standards.

### Quality Standards

- Products must be inspected, repaired, cleaned, tested, and repackaged to meet original manufacturer specifications.
- All items backed by the **Amazon Renewed Guarantee**.
- Amazon conducts test purchases and monitors customer feedback.
- Non-compliance risks deactivation of Renewed privileges.

### Listing Requirements

- Include **(Renewed)** designation in product titles.
- Follow standard Amazon product detail page rules.
- Do not use seller name as brand (except for private label).
- Do not mention warranty in titles or descriptions.

## Amazon Custom

### What It Is

Enables sellers to offer customizable products where customers can personalize, configure, or assemble items to their specifications.

### Customization Types

| Type | Description | Example |
|------|-------------|---------|
| **Personalization** | Customer uploads image or adds text | Engraved jewelry, custom phone cases |
| **Configuration** | Customer selects from seller-provided options | Build-your-own computer, custom furniture finishes |
| **Assembly** | Customer mixes and matches items into bundles | Gift baskets, makeup kits |

### Product Specifications

- Up to **5 customizable surfaces** per product.
- Maximum **15 customizations** per surface.
- Up to **100 total options** per listing.
- Products must be in **new** condition.
- Products are **non-returnable** but may receive refunds/replacements within 30 days for damage or defects.

### Eligibility

- Professional seller ($39.99/month).
- Fee waived for approved Amazon Handmade participants.
- **Cannot use FBA** — requires Fulfilled by Merchant fulfillment.
- Available in most categories except personal computers, laptops, towers, tablets, and video consoles.

### Setup Process

1. Register as a Professional seller.
2. Configure customization options for your products.
3. Fine-tune listings (preview how customization appears to customers).
4. Fulfill orders (customization details accessible through Manage Orders and Order Reports).

Bulk customization updates are available after creating a base ASIN.

## Buy with Prime

### What It Is

A service that lets you offer Prime shopping benefits (fast, free delivery and easy returns) on your **own e-commerce website**, not just on Amazon.

### How It Works

1. Customers visit your website and see the Buy with Prime badge on eligible products.
2. They check out using their Amazon account credentials (payment and shipping info already saved).
3. Amazon fulfills the order from your FBA inventory.
4. Customers get fast, free delivery and easy returns — just like on Amazon.

### Key Benefits

| Benefit | Data |
|---------|------|
| **Conversion increase** | Sellers report up to 40% jump in conversion |
| **New customer acquisition** | 45% of Buy with Prime orders are new customers |
| **Revenue share** | One merchant: Buy with Prime accounts for over 70% of website orders and revenue |
| **Trust signal** | Prime logo and delivery promise build immediate trust |

### Additional Features

- Display Amazon ratings and reviews on your own website.
- Use customer email and order data for your own marketing campaigns.
- Maintain your existing Amazon.com sales simultaneously.

### Requirements

- Create an Amazon Supply Chain account.
- Maintain inventory in Amazon fulfillment centers.
- Products can be exclusive to your website or also sold on Amazon.com.

### Pricing

Usage-based — you only pay for what you use. Per-unit costs depend on product dimensions, weight, average order value, and order quantity. Includes fulfillment, storage, payment processing, and other services. Charges apply only after completing sales (storage costs are ongoing).

## Subscribe & Save

### What It Is

A recurring delivery program where customers set up automatic reorders at discounted prices. Creates reliable, predictable repeat revenue for sellers.

### How It Works

**For customers:** They select a delivery frequency (every 1-6 months) and receive automatic deliveries at a discount.

**For sellers:** Eligible products are typically auto-enrolled once criteria are met. Management happens through the Subscribe & Save dashboard in Seller Central.

### Discount Structure

| Tier | Who Funds | Notes |
|------|-----------|-------|
| **5% base discount** | Amazon-funded | Applied to orders with 5+ subscription items |
| **0% seller discount** | Standard auto-enrollment | No additional seller cost |
| **5% seller discount** | Optional seller-funded | Increases visibility |
| **10% seller discount** | Optional seller-funded | Maximum visibility boost |

**Key stat:** Products with a 10-15% total discount can drive up to a **1.8x increase in conversion**.

### Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Brand Registry** | Brand must be registered; seller needs Brand Representative role |
| **FBA** | Required for auto-enrollment (exceptions via Seller Support) |
| **Professional plan** | Recommended for full access |
| **Product eligibility** | Based on category, price, sales performance, fulfillment history, inventory levels |

### Benefits

- Automates repeat purchases with minimal ongoing effort.
- Builds long-term customer loyalty and brand affinity.
- No additional program fees (beyond standard selling and FBA costs).
- Seller-funded discounts are optional.
- Case study: one brand reports Subscribe & Save representing over **35% of annual revenue**.

## Amazon Vine

### What It Is

A review generation program that connects your products with Amazon's trusted network of reviewers (called "Vine Voices") who provide honest feedback in exchange for free products.

### Key Stat

Sellers using Vine can boost sales an average of **30%**.

### Eligibility Requirements

- **Professional selling account** ($39.99/month).
- **Brand Registry enrollment** (as rights owner, brand representative, or reseller) OR generic products in catalog.
- **FBA-eligible offers** required.
- Product listings must have **fewer than 30 existing reviews**.
- **Ineligible:** Adult items, digital goods, and bundles.

### Pricing

| Units Enrolled | Cost per Parent Product |
|---------------|----------------------|
| **0-2 units** | Free |
| **3-10 units** | $75 |
| **11-30 units** | $200 |

- Child ASINs (variations) incur no additional fees.
- You are charged only after the first review publishes.
- No charge if no reviews appear within 90 days.

### How Vine Voices Work

- Selected based on the insightfulness of their published reviews.
- Amazon matches products with reviewers who have purchase history and category expertise.
- Reviewers receive products free but must report them as income for tax purposes.
- Maximum **30 reviewers** per product enrollment.

### Enrollment Process

1. Log into Seller Central with a Professional account.
2. Enroll your brand in Amazon Brand Registry.
3. Set up FBA for your products.
4. Create customer-ready FBA listings (can enroll before inventory arrives at fulfillment center).
5. Monitor review progress via the Vine dashboard.

### Important Limitations

- **FBA is mandatory** to maintain reviewer anonymity.
- Enrollment **cannot be canceled** once reviewers claim products.
- Enrollment can be halted to prevent additional claims, but this prevents re-enrollment for that product.
- Reviews are honest and unbiased — Vine reviewers are not obligated to write positive reviews.
- Vine reviews are marked with a "Vine Customer Review of Free Product" badge.

### Geographic Availability

Available in 9 markets: US, Canada, UK, France, Germany, Italy, Spain, Australia, Japan.

### New Seller Benefit

Brand Registry enrollment includes a **$200 Vine credit** as part of new seller incentives.
