# Seller Fulfillment on Walmart Marketplace: Complete Guide

## Overview

Walmart Marketplace sellers have multiple fulfillment options for getting products to customers. This guide covers seller-fulfilled shipping requirements, approved carriers, shipping methods, the Walmart+ badge program, fulfillment center setup, and shipping settings configuration.

---

## Shipping & Fulfillment Policy

### Core Requirements

- Sellers must support a minimum of **one shipping method**.
- Choose an appropriate lag time, shipment method, carrier, and carrier service level to meet expected delivery dates.
- Walmart maintains authority to update or extend shipping dates for consistency.

### Operating Schedule
- Support shipments until **11:00 AM local time**, minimum five days weekly.
- Configure active fulfillment center schedules in Seller Center.
- **Additional Days Off** limited to **30 per fulfillment center annually**; excessive use risks suspension or termination.

### Order Cutoff Time
- Default setting is **2:00 PM local time**.
- Sellers can modify this setting.
- Failure to configure properly impacts on-time shipping rates.

### Fulfillment Lag Time
- Default requirement: ship within **two operational days** after receiving an order (unless exemption approved).
- Lag time exemptions are discretionary, revocable, and limited; they depend on the product type.

### Expected Delivery Date
- Calculated from: order cutoff time + lag time + operational days + transit time.
- The date displayed to customers may differ from the internal Expected Delivery Date, incorporating historical On-time Delivery rate and carrier data.

### Expected Ship Date
- Orders are auto-cancelled if not marked as shipped and tracking information has not been uploaded by the Expected Ship Date plus **four calendar days**.

### Delivery Performance Standard
- Maintain minimum **90% or higher** on-time delivery rate.
- Orders not delivered within **five days past the Expected Delivery Date** with no carrier movement in three days trigger auto-cancellation authority for Walmart.

---

## Prohibited Fulfillment Practices

- **No retail arbitrage**: Purchasing from competitors for direct shipment to customers is prohibited.
- **No competitor packaging**: Cannot use competitor boxes or packaging.
- **No third-party identifiers**: Prohibited on packaging, invoices, or packing slips.
- **No promotional materials**: No surveys, review requests, coupons, or marketing items in packages.
- **No phantom shipping**: Cannot mark orders as shipped without physically shipping items.
- **Amazon MCF permitted**: Only when using neutral, unbranded packaging with no competitor branding visible.

## Shipping Methods Overview

Walmart Marketplace sellers have five shipping method options:

### 1. Value Shipping
- Economy shipping option with longer delivery times.
- Lowest cost for sellers.
- Suitable for non-urgent, lower-priced items.

### 2. Standard Shipping
- The most common shipping method.
- Typical delivery time of 3-5 business days depending on origin/destination.
- Required minimum for most sellers.

### 3. TwoDay Shipping
- Delivery within 2 business days.
- Enables the coveted 2-day delivery badge on product listings.
- Significantly increases conversion rates and Buy Box competitiveness.
- Can be achieved via WFS or seller-fulfilled with approved carriers.

### 4. OneDay Shipping
- Next business day delivery.
- Premium shipping option.
- Highest cost but best customer experience.

### 5. Freight Shipping
- For oversized or heavy items.
- Uses freight carriers rather than parcel carriers.
- Longer delivery times typical.

## Approved Parcel Carriers

### Top Preferred Carriers

**USPS (United States Postal Service)**:
- Priority Mail Express: Overnight or 2-day delivery; max 70 lbs, 108 inches length + girth
- Priority Mail: 1-3 business days; offers date-specified delivery
- Retail Ground: 5-7 business days; best for commercial locations; max 70 lbs, 130 inches length + girth
- Coverage: All 50 states and Puerto Rico

**FedEx**:
- FedEx 2Day: 2 business days; up to 150 lbs; 119" length, 165" length plus girth
- FedEx Express Saver: 3 business days
- FedEx Ground/Residential: 1-7 business days depending on origin/destination
- Coverage: All 50 states and Puerto Rico (excludes P.O. boxes)
- Note: FedEx Ground Residential 4-day and 5-day transit lanes currently operate Monday-Friday

**UPS**:
- Next Day Air: 1 business day overnight; ideal for perishables
- 2nd Day Air: 2 business days; economical option
- Ground: 3-5 business days; day-definite delivery in 1 to 5 days
- Coverage: All 50 states and Puerto Rico

## Expedited Delivery

### Two-Day Delivery Eligibility (Seller-Fulfilled)
- Must have been on the Marketplace for more than **90 days**, OR
- Have handled fulfillment for at least **20 orders in the last 30 days** that satisfy performance standards.
- Items must meet specific listing criteria.
- Maintain Seller Performance Standards for the last 30 days.

### Benefits of Two-Day Delivery
- Products display the 2-day delivery badge.
- Increased search visibility and Buy Box competitiveness.
- Higher conversion rates -- customers prefer faster delivery.

## Carrier API Credentials

- API integration with carriers enables automated shipping label generation and tracking.
- Configure carrier API credentials in Seller Center settings.
- Supports automated tracking number upload and shipment status updates.
- Recommended for high-volume sellers managing many orders daily.

## Tracking Requirements

- Provide tracking numbers that are accurate and valid.
- Only communicate tracking to Walmart once a package has been tendered to the carrier for shipment.
- **Valid Tracking Rate**: Maintain **99% or greater** per Seller Performance Standards.
- **Editing Window**: Tracking numbers are editable for up to **four hours** after updating the order to Shipped status.
- Use trackable shipping services with proper carrier documentation.
- Double-check carrier details before submission.
- Provide both carrier name and tracking number.

## Walmart+ Badge Program

### Overview
The Walmart+ badge is a special designation appearing on seller-fulfilled item listings on Walmart.com, indicating fast, reliable shipping to Walmart+ members. Enrolled items receive free shipping for Walmart+ subscribers.

### Eligibility Requirements
- Must have met **Seller Performance Standards** for the last 30 days.
- Items must meet specific listing criteria.
- Items must ship in **three calendar days or less**.
- Verify eligibility on the "Walmart+ Seller Fulfilled" page in Seller Center.

### Enrollment Process
1. Access the **Enroll items in Walmart+** section in Seller Center.
2. Select items to enroll.
3. Submit items for Walmart's review.
4. Walmart reviews enrollment requests and determines final eligibility based on shipping performance metrics.

### Program Details
- **Badges displayed**: Two badges appear under enrolled listings:
  - Walmart+ badge (identifies program participation)
  - "Free with W+" badge (indicates exclusive free shipping for Walmart+ members)
- **Pricing**: Walmart automatically applies free shipping for W+ orders, setting the item's shipping price to zero for those customers. Regular shipping fee configurations apply to all non-Walmart+ orders.
- **Performance monitoring**: The badge may or may not display on a listing as performance metrics change. Enrollment does not guarantee permanent badge display.
- **Flexibility**: You can enroll and unenroll items at any time.

### Tips for Success
- Set up three-calendar-day or faster shipping.
- Use Walmart's OneDay or TwoDay delivery programs.
- Configure lag time to zero using Simplified Shipping Settings.
- Maintain consistent on-time delivery performance.

## Fulfillment Methods

Walmart Marketplace sellers can choose from four fulfillment approaches:

### 1. Seller Fulfillment Services (SFS)
- Sellers maintain complete control by managing their own fulfillment centers.
- Responsible for storing, packing, and shipping items on time from your facility.
- Must add a seller-managed fulfillment center through Seller Center settings.

### 2. Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS)
- Walmart's proprietary end-to-end fulfillment solution.
- Walmart stores inventory, picks, packs, and ships orders on your behalf.
- Handles all customer support and returns.
- Items display "2-Day Shipping" and "Fulfilled by Walmart" tags.
- If approved for Marketplace, you already have access to WFS.
- Fixed monthly storage fee + fulfillment price based on item weight.

### 3. Deliverr (Flexport)
- Third-party logistics partner handling storing and shipping.
- SKUs automatically eligible for Walmart TwoDay Delivery.
- Contact Deliverr directly to establish account, then configure in Seller Center.
- **Important**: If you have used ShipBob or Deliverr in the past, you cannot use the other.

### 4. ShipBob
- Global logistics platform for order fulfillment and shipping.
- Eligible items integrated for Walmart TwoDay Delivery.
- Requires direct outreach to ShipBob before Seller Center configuration.

### Critical Limitation
- Only **one third-party fulfillment partner** is allowed for your entire catalog.
- Partners cannot be used interchangeably.

## Adding Fulfillment Centers

### Seller-Managed Fulfillment Centers
- Navigate to **Settings > Fulfillment** in Seller Center.
- Add fulfillment center details: name, address, operating hours, capabilities.
- Configure active days and operating schedule (minimum 5 days per week).
- Set order cutoff times for each center.
- Additional Days Off limited to 30 per fulfillment center annually.

### Multiple Fulfillment Centers
- Sellers can operate multiple fulfillment centers.
- Each center can have independent operating schedules and capabilities.
- Inventory can be tracked by location across centers.
- Walmart uses location-based inventory to optimize shipping speed and cost.

---

## Shipping Settings

### Simplified Shipping Settings
- Walmart offers a simplified shipping settings interface in Seller Center.
- Configure shipping methods, transit times, and lag times.
- Set shipping prices and free shipping thresholds.

### Shipping Templates
- Create shipping templates to apply consistent shipping settings across items.
- Templates define shipping methods, rates, and transit times.
- Templates can be applied to individual items or groups of items.
- Limited to **10 shipping cost edits per Shipping Template daily**; exceeding this locks the template for 24 hours.

### Shipping Profiles
- Manage shipping settings from the Shipping Profile in your Account page.
- Configure return labels, return centers, and fulfillment center details.
- Post-setup, access and modify through the Account settings.

### Key Configuration Options
- Shipping methods (Value, Standard, TwoDay, OneDay, Freight)
- Transit times per shipping method
- Fulfillment lag time (default: 2 operational days)
- Shipping rates and free shipping thresholds
- Order cutoff times
- Operating days and schedule
- Return shipping configurations
